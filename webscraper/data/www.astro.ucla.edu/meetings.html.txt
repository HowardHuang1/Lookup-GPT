            <h2>
	Keck Science Meeting</h2>
<h3>
	September 19-20, 2019, UCLA Physics &amp; Astronomy</h3>
<div>
	UCLA Physics &amp; Astronomy and the W.M. Keck Observatory are pleased to announce the 2019 Keck Science Meeting, which will take place at UCLA on Thursday and Friday, September 19-20, 2019. The meeting will bring together Keck users from UCLA, Caltech, the University of California, NASA, the University of Hawaii, Yale University, Northwestern, Notre Dame, Swinburne University and Australian Keck users, and Subaru.</div>
<div>
	&nbsp;</div>
<div>
	The primary focus of the meeting will be to share exciting science results made possible by Keck Observatory.</div>

	September 5-7, 2019, UCLA Luskin Conference Center</h3>

<h2>
	NedFest 2017</h2>
<h3>
	August 25-25, 2017 at the UCLA Luskin Conference Center</h3>
<div>
	Nedfest 2017 is a festschrift in honor of Professor Ned Wright’s 70th birthday and his retirement from UCLA. It will be held on August 25-26, 2017 (Friday and Saturday) at UCLA’s Luskin Conference Center and will feature two days of scientific talks highlighting Ned’s impact on a variety of fields in astrophysics and cosmology and on our scientific careers.</div>
<div>
	&nbsp;</div>
<div>
	<strong><a href="https://conferences.pa.ucla.edu/nedfest-2017/index.html">Conference site</a></strong></div>

	<h2>
		Keck Science Meeting</h2>
</div>
<h3>
	September 17-18, 2015 at Physics &amp; Astronomy PAB 1-425.</h3>
<p>UCLA is pleased to host this year's annual meeting of the Keck Observatory user community in the Physics &amp; Astronomy building, room 1-425. The meeting will include two days of both oral and poster presentations from the scientific partners of the Keck Observatory, including Caltech, UC, UH, NASA, TSIP, Yale, Swinburne and WMKO. Participants will share recent, interesting results based on observations conducted with the Keck I and Keck II telescopes on Mauna Kea. A special feature of this meeting will be two sessions on Data Reduction Pipelines. Everyone is invited to attend.</p>
<p>Official Keck Science Meeting website: <strong><a href="https://conferences.pa.ucla.edu/keck/">https://conferences.pa.ucla.edu/keck/</a></strong></p>
