<section class="av_textblock_section "  itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="https://schema.org/CreativeWork" ><div class='avia_textblock  '   itemprop="text" ><h2>ANNOUNCEMENTS:</h2>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">For information regarding <strong>tickets</strong>, please visit our ticketing section, <a href="https://www.college.ucla.edu/commencement/ticketing/">here.</a> Ticketing begins in May 2024.</p>
<h2>GENERAL INFORMATION:</h2>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">UCLA College Commencement and the College’s Academic Department Ceremonies are held from Friday, June 14 to Sunday, June 16, 2024. You will receive information about UCLA College Commencement in <a href="http://my.ucla.edu/directLink.aspx?featureID=33">MyUCLA Notices</a>.  Please keep your <a href="https://my.ucla.edu/directLink.aspx?featureID=231">email address</a> up to date in MyUCLA; this is where your tickets will be delivered. Also, check your spam/junk folders frequently, so that you don’t miss the messages with links to receive the tickets you order.</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">All ceremonies are reserved for graduating students who are eligible to participate.  You are eligible to participate in June Commencement events if:</p>
<ul>
<li>You completed all degree requirements and graduated in Fall 2023 or Winter 2024; or</li>
<li>You are declared to graduate in Spring 2024 or Summer 2024.</li>
</ul>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">A <a href="https://caac.ucla.edu/academic-planning/graduation-commencement/#tab-id-5">Graduation Checklist</a> is available to explain the process of graduating from UCLA and participating in commencement.</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">As a College bachelor’s degree recipient, you will be invited to:</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">The College Commencement Ceremony<br />
Friday, June 14, 2024<br />
11:00 am, 3:00 pm, or 7:00 pm<br />
Pauley Pavilion</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">Academic Department Ceremonies held at various locations across campus during Commencement Weekend. Information about those ceremonies will be available on the <a href="https://commencement.ucla.edu/">campus commencement website</a>.</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">Explore several pages on this website to prepare for commencement.  Above, the June 2024 menu will lead you to <a href="https://www.college.ucla.edu/commencement/ceremony-information/">information about the ceremonies</a>, our <a href="https://www.college.ucla.edu/commencement/ticketing/">ticketing process</a>, <a href="https://www.college.ucla.edu/commencement/disability-accommodation/">disability accommodation</a>, and our <a href="https://www.college.ucla.edu/commencement/faq/">FAQs</a>.</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">
<p style="font-weight: 400;">In the meantime, please be sure to <a href="https://www.uclastore.com/students/students-graduation-resources">order your regalia</a>.  And make sure you’re following the <a href="https://caac.ucla.edu/academic-planning/graduation-commencement/#tab-id-5">Graduation Checklist</a>.</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">
<p style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>Traffic Alert: </strong> During Commencement Weekend, traffic around campus will be very heavy.  Graduates should make plans to be at the ceremony venue at the time indicated on your ticket.  Guests should be prepared to arrive at the ceremony venue at least one hour before the ceremony starts, to be certain they are not delayed by traffic or parking issues.</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">On June 14th, all three ceremonies will be <strong>livestreamed</strong>, and the links will be available on this page and on the campus’s main commencement page:  <a href="https://commencement.ucla.edu/">https://commencement.ucla.edu</a>.</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">

<p style="font-weight: 400;">Please note our <a href="https://www.college.ucla.edu/commencement/finalplanning/">final planning checklist</a>, which has information about Friday, June 14, and the policies for what you can bring into Pauley.</p>
