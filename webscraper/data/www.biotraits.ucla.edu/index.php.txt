<td style="background-color: white; width: 519px; vertical-align: top;"><span style="color: black;">Welcome to the <span style="font-weight: bold;">Global Biotraits Database</span>.<br>
<br>
<a name="introduction"></a>This is an
online resource for empirical data on how biological traits
respond to environmental
drivers such as temperature, light, and salinity.</span><br style="color: black;">
<br>
This database represents the largest and most comprehensive database for the thermal responses of physiological and ecological traits, especially consumer-resource interactions. Currently, we have a total of 2445 intraspecific trait response curves from a total of 1508 species.<br/><span style="color: black; font-weight: bold;"></span><span style="color: black;">We
expect researchers of diverse disciplines to use these <a href="PNAS_fig1.PNG" target="_blank">data</a> to shed
light on how natural
systems respond to environmental drivers.</span><span style="color: black; font-weight: bold;"></span><span style="font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal; letter-spacing: normal; line-height: normal; orphans: 2; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; font-size: medium; display: inline ! important; float: none; color: black;"><br>
<br>
From this site, users will be able to download available datasets and
upload their own data. We hope that this website will become a central
portal for the management and accession of data on the thermal responses
of biological traits.<br><br>We
are currently working through a few bugs with the search capibilities,
but please feel free to try a search. In the meantime, <span style="font-weight: bold;">the</span> <span style="font-weight: bold;">full dataset published in <span style="font-style: italic;">Ecology</span> (2013) can be downloaded </span><a style="font-weight: bold;" target="_blank" href="http://www.esajournals.org/doi/abs/10.1890/12-2060.1">here</a>. <br>
<br>
Work on the website is funded by the <a href="http://www.nsf.gov/awardsearch/showAward.do?AwardNumber=1021010" target="_blank">NSF grant</a> to <a href="http://www.biomath.ucla.edu/vsavage/" target="_blank">Van
Savage</a> and </span><br>
<span style="font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal; letter-spacing: normal; line-height: normal; orphans: 2; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; font-size: medium; display: inline ! important; float: none; color: black;"><a href="https://www.eeb.ucla.edu/Faculty/amarasekare/" target="_blank">Priyanga Amarasekare</a>.</span> <br>
<br>
Please contact <a href="mailto:vsavage@ucla.edu">us</a>
if you have questions, including if you would like
to help out or submit data.<br>
