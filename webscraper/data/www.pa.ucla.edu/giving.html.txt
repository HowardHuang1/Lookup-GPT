      <h2>Donating to the Department</h2>
      <br />
      <p><!--<img src="profs.jpg" class="image1 float-left" hspace="15px" alt="...">-->
      <a href="https://giving.ucla.edu/Campaign/Donate.aspx?SiteNum=62">The Physics and Astronomy Chair's Discretionary Fund</a> is an extremely valuable source of funds for 
      our department, permitting flexible, timely, and effective responses to the challenges of maintaining a world-class teaching and research environment. 
      It directly supports many important initiatives that are unique to our department and have a wide-ranging impact on our students, 
      faculty and also on the community. The link allows you to make your donation in honor or memory of someone.</p>
      <p>Other opportunities include creating graduate student fellowships, distinguished ("named") postdoctoral fellow appointments, providing a permanent fund for maintenance 
      of the planetarium theater, developing a permanent endowment of summer research funds for our undergraduates, and purchasing a state-of-the-art computer-controlled mill 
      for the Machine Shop/ Instrument Fabrication Facility.  In addition, all research groups such as biological physics, the galactic center group, the particle theory group, 
      and others would appreciate your support. Please contact <a href="mailto:chair@physics.ucla.edu">chair@physics.ucla.edu</a> if you are interested.</p>
