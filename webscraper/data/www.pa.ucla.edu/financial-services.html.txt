      <h4>Purchasing</h4>
      <p><a href="mailto:orders@physics.ucla.edu">Please submit your ROF orders here</a>. Although most orders are processed the same day, please allow a 3-day turn around time when placing your orders.  
      Same day "urgent" requests must be in by 12:00 noon. The subject line on the email should include the vendor name (s), e.g. Fisher/Praxair.</p>
      <p>Purchasing forms must be completely filled out (i.e. vendor name, address, phone/fax, funding info, etc.) in order to expedite the processing of your 
      order.  Please see ROF instructions at right for additional information.</p>
