  <h1 class="article-header-title">Over three dozen UCLA faculty listed among world’s most influential researchers</h1>

      <div class="article-body text-editor"><p><span><span><span></span></span></span></p>

<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>Forty UCLA faculty members have been named among the world&rsquo;s most influential researchers in the sciences and social sciences.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>

<p><span><span><span><span><span><span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p>

<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>The&nbsp;<a href="https://clarivate.com/highly-cited-researchers/" target="_blank"><span>Highly Cited Researchers list,</span></a> compiled annually by analytics firm Clarivate, identifies scholars whose work has been cited most often in papers published by other researchers in their fields over the past decade. Those chosen for the 2023 list have authored studies that rank in the top 1% in the number of scholarly citations worldwide.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>

<p><span><span><span><span><span><span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p>

<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>&ldquo;The Highly Cited Researchers list identifies and celebrates exceptional individual researchers at UCLA whose significant and broad influence in their fields translates to impact in their research community and innovations that make the world healthier, more sustainable and more secure,&rdquo; said David Pendlebury, head of research analysis at Clavariate&rsquo;s Institute for Scientific Information. &ldquo;Their contributions resonate far beyond their individual achievements, strengthening the foundation of excellence and innovation in research.&rdquo; </span></span></span></span></span></span></p>

<p><span><span><span><span><span><span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p>

<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>UCLA faculty members named to the list, along with their primary UCLA research field or fields, are:</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>

<p><span><span><span><span><span><span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p>

<p><span><span><span><span><span><a href="https://samueli.ucla.edu/people/nasim-annabi/" target="_blank"><span>Nasim Annabi,</span></a>&nbsp;chemical and biomolecular engineering</span></span></span></span></span></p>

<p><span><span><span><span><span><a href="https://bri.ucla.edu/people/carrie-bearden/" target="_blank"><span>Carrie Bearden,</span></a> psychiatry and biobehavioral sciences</span></span></span></span></span></p>

<p><span><span><span><span><span><a href="https://people.healthsciences.ucla.edu/institution/personnel?personnel_id=2068345" target="_blank"><span>Matthew Budoff,</span></a>&nbsp;medicine</span></span></span></span></span></p>

<p><span><span><span><span><span><a href="https://samueli.ucla.edu/people/jun-chen/" target="_blank"><span>Jun Chen,</span></a>&nbsp;bioengineering</span></span></span></span></span></p>

<p><span><span><span><span><span><a href="https://www.mimg.ucla.edu/people/genhongcheng/" target="_blank"><span>Genhong Cheng,</span></a> microbiology, immunology and molecular genetics</span></span></span></span></span></p>

<p><span><span><span><span><span><a href="https://www.uclahealth.org/cancer/members/bartosz-chmielowski" target="_blank"><span>Bartosz Chmielowski,</span></a>&nbsp;medicine</span></span></span></span></span></p>

<p><span><span><span><span><span><a href="https://www.uclahealth.org/providers/timothy-cloughesy" target="_blank"><span>Timothy Cloughesy,</span></a> medicine</span></span></span></span></span></p>

<p><span><span><span><span><span><a href="https://www.semel.ucla.edu/profile/giovanni-coppola-md" target="_blank"><span>Giovanni Coppola,</span></a>&nbsp;psychiatry and biobehavioral sciences</span></span></span></span></span></p>

<p><span><span><span><span><span><a href="https://www.psych.ucla.edu/faculty-page/mcraske/" target="_blank"><span>Michelle Craske,</span></a> psychology</span></span></span></span></span></p>
