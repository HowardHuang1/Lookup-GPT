      <section class="section-text">
    <p>Visiting Graduate Researchers (VGRs) are domestic or international degree-seeking graduate students at their home institution who are invited to conduct short-term doctoral research or participate in a mentored or independent research project or master’s research collaborations with a faculty member at UCLA. The initial Visiting Graduate Researcher appointment can range from 3 weeks to 12 months; the maximum stay is 24 months. Please visit Graduate Division’s website for <a href="https://grad.ucla.edu/academics/research/visiting-graduate-researchers/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Visiting Graduate Researchers</a> for complete information on applying, procedures and training, and calendar and fees. If you have any questions after reviewing Graduate Division’s website, please email: <a href="mailto:&#x77;&#97;&#x63;&#100;&#97;&#x64;&#109;&#105;&#x73;&#115;&#x69;&#x6f;&#x6e;&#x73;&#x40;&#x61;&#114;&#x74;&#x73;&#x2e;&#117;&#99;&#108;&#97;&#x2e;&#101;&#100;&#x75;">wacdadmissions@arts.ucla.edu</a>.</p>  </section>
