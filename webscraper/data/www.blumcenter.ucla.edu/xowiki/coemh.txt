        <p><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 18.2399997711182px;">The UCLA Blum Center partners closely with <a href="http://www.ucghi.universityofcalifornia.edu/coes/migration-and-health/mission.aspx" target="_blank" title="">The Center of Expertise on Migration and Health (COEMH),</a> part of the <a href="http://ucghi.universityofcalifornia.edu" target="_blank" title="">University of California Global Health Institute (UCGHI)</a>. Leveraging talent at all 10 UC campuses, the two centers work synergistically on global health issues impacting Latino immigrants and their communities.&nbsp;</span>
        </p>
        <p>&nbsp;<strong style="color: rgb(31, 73, 125); font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 21.3333339691162px; line-height: 24.3199996948242px;">Vision</strong>
        </p>
        <p>&nbsp;<span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; line-height: 18.2399997711182px;">Worldwide populations in movement will have&nbsp;<font color="#31849b"><strong>health&nbsp;</strong></font></span><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; line-height: 18.2399997711182px;">and</span><strong style="font-size: 16px; color: rgb(49, 132, 155); font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; line-height: 18.2399997711182px;">&nbsp;dignity</strong><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 18.2399997711182px;">.</span>
        </p>
        <p><strong style="font-size: 21.3333339691162px; line-height: 24.3199996948242px; color: rgb(31, 73, 125); font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">Mission&nbsp;</strong>
        </p>
        <div id="WordSection1">
          <p><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 18.2399997711182px;">COEMH provides leadership to improve health equity of migrants and refugees around the world. We conduct action-oriented research and policy analyses, create learning opportunities, and disseminate knowledge using innovative methods. We draw upon multidisciplinary scholarship from all UC campuses and our international collaborators. &nbsp; &nbsp;</span>
          </p>
          <p><strong style="font-size: 21.3333339691162px; line-height: 24.3199996948242px; color: rgb(31, 73, 125); font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">Key Features</strong>
          </p>
          <p>&nbsp;
          </p>
          <ul>
            <li><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 18.2399997711182px;">A multi-campus, multi-disciplinary&nbsp;<strong style="color: rgb(49, 132, 155); line-height: 18.2399997711182px;">network</strong>&nbsp;of experts, students and partners engaged in&nbsp;<strong style="color: rgb(49, 132, 155); line-height: 18.2399997711182px;">community-based</strong>&nbsp;research, education and policy innovations.</span></li>
            <li><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 18.2399997711182px;"><strong style="color: rgb(49, 132, 155); line-height: 18.2399997711182px;">Hands-on, action oriented&nbsp;</strong>learning experiences related to migrant health issues through fieldwork placements in migrants&#39; communities of origin and destination as well as NGOs and public agencies that interact with immigrant and refugee populations. </span></li>
            <li><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 18.2399997711182px;">Opportunities for&nbsp;<strong style="color: rgb(49, 132, 155); line-height: 18.2399997711182px;">transnational collaboration</strong>&nbsp;between UC and internationally-based researchers. </span></li>
            <li><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 18.2399997711182px;"><strong style="color: rgb(49, 132, 155); line-height: 18.2399997711182px;">Support&nbsp;</strong>for scholars, faculty and students to develop and showcase work in the field of migration and health. </span>&nbsp;</li>
          </ul>
