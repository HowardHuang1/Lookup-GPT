			<h1 class="pb-24">Degree Programs, Majors and Minors</h1>

			<p class="p--lead-in">UCLA offers a vast array of degree programs and areas of study for undergraduate and graduate students. Your academic pursuits can flex to suit your needs. You can study abroad, pursue a minor in addition to your major or even earn two degrees at once. In some fields, you can pursue a master's degree as an undergraduate. </p>

			<p class="pb-2">Use the tool below to learn about the degrees, majors and minors that UCLA offers. Select the degrees that interest you to learn about their specific requirements.</p>

			<p class="pb-2">Some degree programs offer specializations that help you hone in on your specific area of interest. For example, you can earn an M.F.A. in film and television with a specialization in screenwriting, an M.M. in music performance with a specialization in jazz, or an M.S. in biology with a specialization in the ecology and evolution of medicine. Visit the departments for the programs that interest you to learn more.</p>

			<p class="pb-2">If you're unsure what you want to pursue, attend <a href="https://newstudents.ucla.edu/">New Student Orientation</a> the summer before you start at UCLA. You'll learn about choosing a major, course planning, graduation requirements, graduate programs, professional schools and more.</p>
