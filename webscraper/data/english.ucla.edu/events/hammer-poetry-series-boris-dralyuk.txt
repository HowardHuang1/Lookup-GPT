								<h2>Hammer Poetry Series: Boris Dralyuk</h2>
								
								<div class="text no-padding">
																		<strong><em>My Hollywood and Other Poems</em></strong><br>
																		<strong>When:</strong> Thursday, February 22, 2024 7:30 pm<br>
																		<strong>Where:</strong> Hammer Museum																	</div>
															<img src="https://english.ucla.edu/wp-content/uploads/Boris-Dralyuk-hero_1.jpg" class="img" />	
															<div class="text"><p>Poet <strong>Boris Dralyuk</strong> reads from his most recent volume, <em>My Hollywood and Other Poems</em>, a collection of lyric meditations on the experience of émigrés in Los Angeles. The Poetry Foundation proclaimed that “the ache of exile reverberates against the irretrievability of the past” in the Odessa-born poet’s work.</p>
<div class="paragraph paragraph--text paragraph--type--text-paragraph paragraph--view-mode--default">
<div class="clearfix text-formatted field field--name-field-paragraph-text field--type-text-long field--label-hidden field__item">
<p>Organized and hosted by poet, literary critic, and UCLA Distinguished Research Professor <strong>Stephen Yenser</strong>. Cosponsored by UCLA English and UCLA Recreation.</p>
<p>Limited books available for purchase.</p>
<p><strong>Questions about the event?</strong><br />
Visit the <a href="https://hammer.ucla.edu/programs-events/2024/poetry-boris-dralyuk">Hammer Museum</a> for full details.</p>
