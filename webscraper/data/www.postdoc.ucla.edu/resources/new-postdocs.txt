<h2 id="page-header">Information for New Postdoctoral Scholars</h2>
            
<div class="section clear-fix">
    <h2>Get Connected!</h2>
<p>There are a number of ways to <a href="https://www.postdoc.ucla.edu/connect/">connect to the postdoctoral scholar community</a> at UCLA. Among the most effective ways to learn about events and information relevant to your postdoctoral appointment is to sign up for the <a href="http://eepurl.com/dGodRz">UCLA Postdoctoral Scholar Mailing List</a>.</p>
<h2>Mandatory New Postdoctoral Scholar Orientation</h2>
<p style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>Join us on the first Tuesday of every month (see orientation dates below) from 4:00 PM to 5:00 PM in MacDonald Research Laboratories (MRL) Room 1-441</strong><strong> for the New Postdoctoral Scholar Orientation </strong>(675 Charles E Young Drive South, L<span class="locality">os Angeles</span>, <span class="region">CA</span> <span class="postal-code">90095</span>)<strong>. Please note that the orientation is <u>mandatory for all new postdoctoral scholars</u>.</strong> Postdocs who have already attended an orientation do not need to attend.</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">If you cannot attend this orientation, <a href="http://uaw5810.org/orientations/">please complete this form</a> to schedule a make-up time for your mandatory union orientation.</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">The orientation will include a Q&amp;A regarding campus resources and a brief presentation by the Postdoctoral Association (PDA at UCLA). Postdoc representatives from the postdoc union (<a href="https://uaw5810.org/">UAW Local 5810</a>) will answer questions about the postdoctoral contract and union membership.</p>
<p><strong>2024 Orientation Dates (more dates to be announced soon)</strong></p>
<p>January 9 (Date adjusted because of Winter Closure)<br />
February 6<br />
March 5</p>
<p>Subscribe to our <a href="http://eepurl.com/dGodRz">UCLA Postdoctoral Scholar Mailing List</a> for announcements about upcoming orientations!</p>
<h3>Orientation Resources</h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="https://youtu.be/URwKaYqyVmc">UCLA Postdoc Policy: Top 10 Most Frequently Asked Postdoc Questions</a> (recording)</li>

</ul>
<p>If you have any questions about the orientation, please contact <a href="mailto:askgrad@grad.ucla.edu">askgrad@grad.ucla.edu</a>.</p>
<h2>Appointment Paperwork and Information</h2>
<p>For information about required paperwork associated with your postdoctoral appointment, please view the <a href="https://www.postdoc.ucla.edu/resources/appointment-information/appointment-paperwork/">Appointment Procedures</a> page and contact your Department Administrator. For information about your appointment, please view the <a href="https://www.postdoc.ucla.edu/resources/appointment-information/">Appointment Policy and Procedures</a> page.</p>
