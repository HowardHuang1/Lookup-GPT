  <h1 class="page-title"><span class="field field--name-title field--type-string field--label-hidden">How To Add A Contingent Worker to UCPath</span>
</h1>


  </div>

  </div>

      </div>
    
    <div class="layout__content">
        <div class="region region-content" aria-label="content">
    
<div id="block-siteden-surface-content" class="block block-system block-system-main-block panel">
    
    
        
<article role="article" about="/how-add-contingent-worker-ucpath" class="node node--type-sf-page node--view-mode-full">

  
    

  
  <div class="node__content">
    
      <div class="field field--name-field-sf-content field--type-entity-reference-revisions field--label-hidden field__items">
              <div class="field__item">  <div class="paragraph paragraph--type--sf-section paragraph--view-mode--default">
            <div class="layout layout--onecol">
    <div  class="layout__region layout__region--content">
        <div class="paragraph paragraph--type--sf-text paragraph--view-mode--default">
          
            <div class="clearfix text-formatted field field--name-field-sf-wysiwyg field--type-text-long field--label-hidden field__item"><div>
<div>
<div>
<div>
<div>
<div>Refer to the<span> </span><a href="https://www.cru.ucla.edu/processing-calendars" target="_blank">CRU and UCPath pay processing calendars</a><span> </span>to determine the deadlines for submitting template transactions.<br /><strong> </strong><br /><strong>STEP 1 – Create Position</strong></div>

<ul><li>If not already available, create the position for the appropriate CWR job code.</li>
</ul><div> <br />
Follow these step-by-step instructions to<span> </span><a href="https://sp.ucop.edu/sites/ucpathhelp/LocationUsers/LOCplayer/data/printit/8690c5d0-f2e6-41fd-bdc8-45ddc3d52b51_SPD.pdf" target="_blank">Initiate New Position Control Request</a><span> </span>in UCPath.</div>

<div> </div>

<div>Note: CWR positions can be single head count or multiple head count as they are not represented or part of the permanent budget.</div>

<ol><li>Navigate to Position Control<span> </span><em>(PeopleSoft Menu &gt; UC Customizations &gt; UC Extensions &gt;<span> </span></em><strong><em>Position Control Request</em></strong><em>)</em></li>
	<li>Enter the following information:</li>
</ol><ul><li><strong>Effective Date:</strong><span> </span>Enter the effective date for the transaction. Note: Do not use the same date as the job effective date. Best practice: enter at least 30 days prior to job effective date.</li>
	<li><strong>Status:</strong><span> </span>Active (default)</li>
	<li><strong>Reason:</strong><span> </span>NEW (default)</li>
	<li><strong>Position Status:</strong><span> </span>Approved (default)</li>
	<li><strong>Job Code:</strong><span> </span>Select the appropriate<span> </span><a href="https://sp.ucop.edu/sites/ucpathhelp/LocationUsers/LOCjobaids/UCPC_PHCMWFAL250JA_CWRJobCodesAndDescriptions_D2Rev00.pdf" target="_blank">CWR job code</a>.</li>
	<li><strong>Full/Part Time:<span> </span></strong>Variable</li>
	<li><strong>Union:</strong><span> </span>Enter<span> </span><strong><em>ZZZ</em></strong><span> </span><em>– No Union code-CWR</em></li>
	<li><strong>Department:</strong><span> </span>Enter the department code the CWR should be associated with</li>
	<li><strong>Location:</strong><span> </span>Accept the default from Department or select the appropriate location from the dropdown</li>
	<li><strong>Reports To:<span> </span></strong>Enter the position number of the Reports To</li>
	<li><strong>Salary Admin Plan:</strong><span> </span>Leave Blank</li>
	<li><strong>Salary Grade</strong>: Leave Blank</li>
	<li><strong>Max Head Count:</strong><span> </span>Enter the number of incumbents to be added to the position</li>
	<li><strong>FTE:</strong><span> </span>Enter the appropriate FTE</li>
	<li><strong>HR Worksite:<span> </span></strong>Enter<span> </span><strong><em>N<span> </span></em></strong><em>– No I9 Integration Required</em></li>
	<li><strong>Employee Relations Code:</strong><span> </span><em>Not applicable – Contingent Wk</em></li>
	<li><strong>Submit for approval</strong></li>
</ul><div><strong>Note:</strong><span> </span>You may get the following warning message, simply select “OK” to submit.</div>

<div><br />
Figure 1- Position Control CWR warning message</div>

<div> </div>

<div> </div>

<div><strong>STEP 2 – Submit Add CWR POSN Template</strong></div>

<ul><li>Add your Contingent Worker/s into the new position/s</li>
</ul><div> <br />
Follow these step-by-step instructions to<span> </span><a href="https://sp.ucop.edu/sites/ucpathhelp/LocationUsers/LOCplayer/data/printit/f217c8b2-a74d-48b6-950f-9f9575dff721_SPD.pdf" target="_blank">Initiate Add Contingent Worker (With Position) Template Transaction</a></div>

<ol><li>Navigate to Smart HR Transactions<span> </span><em>(PeopleSoft Menu &gt; Workforce Administration &gt; Smart HR Template &gt;<span> </span></em><strong><em>Smart HR Transactions</em></strong><em>)</em></li>
	<li>Enter the following information:</li>
</ol><ul><li><strong>Template:</strong><span> </span>UC_ADD_CWR_POSN</li>
</ul><ul><li><strong>Effective Date:</strong><span> </span>Enter effective date for the transaction</li>
	<li><strong>Reason Code:</strong><span> </span>Add Contingent Worker (default)</li>
	<li><strong>Name:</strong><span> </span>Required</li>
	<li><strong>Date of Birth:<span> </span></strong><em>Required</em><strong><span> </span></strong>for campus UID creation</li>
	<li><strong>National ID (SSN):<span> </span></strong><em>Essential<span> </span></em>for campus UID creation unless CWR is a foreign national and has not been issued a Social Security Number. If the CWR has not been issued an SSN, leave this field blank and note why in the Initiator Comments section. </li>
	<li><strong>Person Phone Number</strong>:</li>
	<li><strong>Person Email Address</strong>: A “Home” email is required</li>
	<li>Patent Acknowledgement Sign Dt: Enter the date if paper completed.<span> </span><strong><em>Note:</em></strong><span> </span>CWRs are required to complete the paper or electronic Patent Acknowledgment.</li>
	<li><strong>UC External System ID</strong>: Always leave this section blank</li>
	<li><strong>Position Number:</strong><span> </span>Enter the Position Number of the CWR Position (ex. CWR003, CWR015, CWR020, etc.) you created as part of Step 1 of this process</li>
	<li><strong>Employee Classification:</strong><span> </span>Select “13” if Staff CWR job code or “14” if an Academic CWR job code</li>
	<li><strong>Expected Job End Date:</strong><span> </span>Enter the end date of the CWR job</li>
	<li><strong>Initiators Comments:<span> </span></strong>Enter the appropriate comments for the transaction</li>
	<li><strong>Save and Submit</strong></li>
</ul><div><strong> </strong><br />
Any questions pertaining to<span> </span><strong>transacting in UCPath<span> </span></strong>can be directed to the Central Resource Unit as a case<span> </span><a href="https://request.finance.ucla.edu/secure/?frm_id=HRAdminRequest" target="_blank">here</a>.</div>
</div>
