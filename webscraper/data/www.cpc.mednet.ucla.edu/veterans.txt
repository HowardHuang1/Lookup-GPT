          <p>
	Our Emergency Medical Technician (EMT) and Paramedic Education courses are approved by the Department of Veterans Affairs (DVA) to certify enrollment for eligible veterans for the GI Bill®.</p>
<p>
	As a veteran, you must follow and meet the EMT and Paramedic courses' established eligibility, enrollment, and tuition payment requirements. Please note that fee payments for students enrolling in the EMT and Paramedic courses are&nbsp;due prior to the start of the course. The DVA pays eligible veterans their designated rate throughout their enrollment in the course. (For purposes of the VA, the EMT and Paramedic courses are considered clock-hour programs. The VA typically only reimburses students the cost of fees.)</p>
<p>
	If you have the Post-9/11 GI Bill, you may be able to sign up for a course using our GI Bill Student Enrollment Agreement if the VA will pre-pay your course.</p>
<p>
	If you are a veteran and are eligible to receive Veterans Benefits, please visit the <a href="http://gibill.va.gov/">DVA web site</a> or call the DVA at 1-888-442-4551&nbsp;for questions about eligibility, processing your benefits, and obtaining a Certificate of Eligibility.</p>
<p>
	Here is what you need to have in hand before setting up your appointment with UCLA Center for Prehospital Care Certifying School Official:</p>
<ol>
<li>
<p>
			<strong>Your current Certificate of Eligibility letter</strong> from the VA.<br />
			(You can call them to request one at&nbsp;888-GI-BILL-1.)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>
			<strong>A&nbsp;copy of your DD-214</strong> (Member Copy 4 showing your discharge status).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>
			<strong>Unofficial or official transcripts</strong> from any schools you attended&nbsp;<em>after </em>high school.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>
			<strong>The name and&nbsp;address of the last school</strong> where you used GI Bill (if you have used it before).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>
			<strong>If you paid into a “Kicker” program</strong> (i.e., if you paid extra funds into the GI Bill) you should bring that paperwork if you have it.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>
	<strong>Paramedic students:</strong>&nbsp;Please refer to your acceptance letter for details on using your Veterans Benefits to pay for your course. The letter will tell you when and where to send your documentation.</p>
<p>
	For questions about using Veterans Benefits in our programs, please contact our School Certifying Official, Darlene Karim, at <a href="mailto:dkarim@mednet.ucla.edu">dkarim@mednet.ucla.edu</a>.</p>
<p>
	<em>GI Bill® is a registered trademark of the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs (VA). More information about education benefits offered by VA is available at the official U.S. government website at <a href="http://www.benefits.va.gov/gibill" target="_blank">http://www.benefits.va.gov/gibill</a>.</em></p>
