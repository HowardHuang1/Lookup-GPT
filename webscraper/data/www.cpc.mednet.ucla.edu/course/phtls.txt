<p style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); font-size: 11pt; font-family: Calibri, sans-serif; margin: 0px;">
	<span style="font-size:14px;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="color:#696969;"><span style="border: 0px; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-optical-sizing: inherit; font-kerning: inherit; font-feature-settings: inherit; font-variation-settings: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: baseline;">NAEMT's Prehospital Trauma Life Support (PHTLS) is recognized around the world as the leading continuing education program for prehospital emergency trauma care.&nbsp; The mission of PHTLS is&nbsp;<i>to promote excellence in trauma patient management by all providers involved in the delivery of prehospital care</i>. &nbsp;</span></span></span></span></p>
<p style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); font-size: 11pt; font-family: Calibri, sans-serif; margin: 0px;">
	<span style="font-size:14px;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="color:#696969;"><span style="border: 0px; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-optical-sizing: inherit; font-kerning: inherit; font-feature-settings: inherit; font-variation-settings: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: baseline;">&nbsp;</span></span></span></span></p>
<p style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); font-size: 11pt; font-family: Calibri, sans-serif; margin: 0px;">
	<span style="font-size:14px;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="color:#696969;"><span style="border: 0px; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-optical-sizing: inherit; font-kerning: inherit; font-feature-settings: inherit; font-variation-settings: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: baseline;">PHTLS courses improve the quality of trauma care and decrease mortality. The program is based on a philosophy stressing the treatment of the multi-system trauma patient as a unique entity with specific needs.&nbsp; PHTLS promotes critical thinking as the foundation for providing quality care. It is based on the belief that, given a good fund of knowledge and key principles, EMS practitioners are capable of making reasoned decisions regarding patient care.&nbsp; This PHTLS course&nbsp;<span style="border: 1pt none windowtext; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-optical-sizing: inherit; font-kerning: inherit; font-feature-settings: inherit; font-variation-settings: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: baseline;">is appropriate for EMTs, paramedics, nurses, physician assistants, physicians, and other prehospital providers.&nbsp;&nbsp;</span>&nbsp;</span></span></span></span></p>
<p style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); font-size: 11pt; font-family: Calibri, sans-serif; margin: 0px;">
	<span style="font-size:14px;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="color:#696969;"><span style="border: 0px; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-optical-sizing: inherit; font-kerning: inherit; font-feature-settings: inherit; font-variation-settings: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: baseline;">&nbsp;</span></span></span></span></p>
<p style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); font-size: 11pt; font-family: Calibri, sans-serif; margin: 0px;">
	<span style="font-size:14px;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="color:#696969;"><span style="border: 1pt none windowtext; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-optical-sizing: inherit; font-kerning: inherit; font-feature-settings: inherit; font-variation-settings: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: baseline;">This two-day course combines lecture with skill scenario stations to allow students the opportunity&nbsp;to apply the material they learn from lecture.&nbsp;</span><span style="border: 0px; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-optical-sizing: inherit; font-kerning: inherit; font-feature-settings: inherit; font-variation-settings: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: baseline;">The course is accredited by CAPCE for 16 hours of continuing education.&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></span></span></span></p>
