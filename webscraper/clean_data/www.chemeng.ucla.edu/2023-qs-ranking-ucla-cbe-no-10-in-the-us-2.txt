2023 QS Ranking: UCLA CBE No. 10 in the US
Mar 22, 2023
In the just-released and broadly watched 2023 QS ranking of Chemical Engineering Departments, our Department was ranked at No. 10 in the US. In addition to reputation surveys of academics and employers, this ranking analyzes publications and citations to the works coming out of our Department. Overall, our School of Engineering was ranked at No. 8 in the US. Other Departments in the School were ranked in the US as follows: Computer Science (9), Civil and Environmental Engineering (10), Electrical and Computer Engineering (7) and Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering (13).
