In accordance with UCLA and public health guidance, we are continuing to operate courses using COVID-19 safety precautions.
Students enrolled in courses that have been canceled will be given the option to transfer at no cost or to be issued a full refund.
Students enrolled in courses must request a transfer or refund up to 14 days before the start day of the course. Non-refundable fees and transfer fees apply. Make your requests in writing to
cpc@mednet.ucla.edu
.
The above policy does not apply to the Paramedic primary course. Student in the Paramedic primary course should refer to the transfer refund policy included in their acceptance package.
If you have any questions please contact us by e-mail at
cpc@mednet.ucla.edu
or give us a call at (310) 267-5959.
