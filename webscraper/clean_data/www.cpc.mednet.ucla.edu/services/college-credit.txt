<p>
	The process is briefly summarized:</p>
<ol>
<li>
		You must become a California Community College student for these units to be recorded. Complete <a href="http://www.laccd.edu/Students/opencccapply/applywlac/Pages/default.aspx" target="_blank">WLAC’s online application</a> and make sure you enroll for the current semester.</li>
</ol>
<ol>
<li value="2">
		Fill out the WLAC request for prior learning form (at <a href="http://www.wlac.edu/Academic-Affairs/Credit-for-Prior-Learning.aspx" target="_blank">WLAC's Credit for Prior Learning page</a>) and follow the instructions on the form.</li>
</ol>
<ol>
<li value="3">
		Request an official copy of your transcript from UCLA by filling out the <a href="https://www.cpc.mednet.ucla.edu/sites/default/files/attached_files/FAQ%20%26%20Transcript%20Req%20Form%20Final%20030623%20%281%29.pdf">UCLA transcript request form</a> and request the transcript to be sent to WLAC at: <a href="mailto:transcripts@wlac.edu">transcripts@wlac.edu</a>.</li>
</ol>
<ol>
<li value="4">
		This process will take an average of 6-8 weeks and is subject to approval by WLAC. UCLA is unable to approve or expedite this process or requests.</li>
</ol>
<h2>
	Continuing Education Units (CEUs)</h2>
<p>
	The UCLA Center for Prehospital Care is an approved Continuing Education Provider for the California EMS (CEP #19-0307) and the California Board of Registered Nursing (CEP #12416). Continuing education credit is available for most courses. The number of continuing education (CE) hours and objectives for a course are available upon request.</p>
