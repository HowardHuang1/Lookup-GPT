Paramedic Refresher
The Paramedic Refresher course meets the 30-hour National Component requirement for renewing your National Registry certification. It also meets the California Paramedic Recertification CEU requirement of 48 hours.

Not yet an EMT?
If you are just starting your career in EMS, step into LA’s top EMT Program at UCLA. After six months of full-time EMT work experience, you’ll qualify to apply to UCLA’s Paramedic Program.
