Answer the Call.
YOU ARE NEEDED MORE THAN EVER!
Emergency medical services (EMS) are a critical component of the nation’s healthcare system. Communities rely on the well-trained workforce of EMS providers for help in everyday emergencies, large-scale incidents and natural disasters alike. EMS saves lives.
At the Center for Prehospital Care, we are committed to providing the best EMS education we can. As a student, you will receive quality education that will prepare you for critical roles throughout the healthcare system. Answer the call and be part of a history of excellence and service. You are needed now, more than ever.
