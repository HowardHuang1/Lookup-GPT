Trauma & NAEMT Courses
We offer a comprehensive selection of EMS and prehospital care courses. Our course areas include:
Emergency Medical Technician: Primary EMT training in several schedules, including an online/hybrid program, as well as preparation and recertification courses.
Paramedic: Full-time and part-time Paramedic education program, including preparation, refresher, and practical skills courses.
CPR, BLS, ACLS, PALS: Our comprehensive selection of AHA life-support programs includes both online and traditional courses.
Phlebotomy: Primary training for Phlebotomy certification in California.
Special Programs: These include specialized trauma courses such as Prehospital Trauma Life Support (PHTLS), Trauma Nurse Core Course, and a variety of instructor courses.
For Organizations: We work with a number of local hospitals, ambulance companies, fire, and law-enforcement agencies to provide ongoing internal quality improvement and training for providers. We can also design custom programs to meet your organization's particular goals.
