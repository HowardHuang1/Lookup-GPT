Attend a webinar
We offer a variety of general and program-specific
Information Session
webinars throughout the summer and fall to help you prepare for admission.
Attend a Virtual Q&A
Have a question about one of our programs? Admission staff host hour-long virtual Q&A sessions catered to each program. A list of sessions and additional details are coming soon. No RSVP is required.
Schedule an Admission Counseling Appointment
We can meet one-on-one by phone or Zoom to review and discuss admission requirements and provide additional information. Appointments are 15 minutes in length. We encourage you to attend an information session webinar before a counseling session.
Prospective students can book a session through
Microsoft Bookings
to schedule an admissions counseling appointment.
