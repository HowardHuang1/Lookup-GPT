Directions to UCLA School of Nursing
The UCLA School of Nursing is located at:
UCLA School of Nursing
700 Tiverton Avenue
Los Angeles, CA 90095
The UCLA School of Nursing is located in the Factor Building on campus near the Geffen School of Medicine and the School of Dentistry.
From LAX to Main Campus:
From LAX, take the 405 Freeway north to the Wilshire Blvd. East exit. Continue east on Wilshire for several blocks, moving into the left lane. Make a left turn on Westwood Blvd. and follow it into campus. UCLA's main campus is bounded by Sunset Blvd. on the north and Le Conte Ave. on the south; the east border is Hilgard Ave. and the west border is Gayley Ave.
From 405 Freeway South:
Take the 405 Freeway south to the Wilshire Blvd. East exit.  Merge onto Wilshire Blvd., turn left at Gayley Ave., turn right at Le Conte Ave., and then turn left at Tiverton Ave. to arrive to the Factor Building.
