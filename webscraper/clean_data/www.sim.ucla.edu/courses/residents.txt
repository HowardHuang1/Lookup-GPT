Resident / Fellow Simulation Training
The UCLA Simulation Center is a core medical training facility for physician training within the UCLA Health System hospitals, including the
Ronald Reagan UCLA Medical Center
,
West Los Angeles VA Medical Center
,
Santa Monica Medical Center
,
Olive View-UCLA Medical Center
, and others. Resident and fellows from a range of medical disciplines frequently utilize our facilities for simulation training and independent practice.
Courses for Residents / Fellows
Residents come to the UCLA Simulation Center for simulation sessions integrated within their respective residency training programs, including the following:
Anesthesiology Critical Incident Simulation Training
Internal Medicine Simulation Training
Emergency Medicine Simulation Training
Family Medicine Simulation Training
Pediatric Simulation Training
Neonatology Simulation Training
OBGYN Simulation Training
Radiology Resident Simulation Training
Surgery
Certification in BLS, ACLS and ATLS
Interprofessional team training simulations for the OR, L&D, ED, NICU
Resources for Residents / Fellows
If you are a resident or fellow interested in exploring simulation training, reserving space in Rosenfeld Hall, conducting research that involves simulation, or becoming a simulation instructor, please contact us by email at
sim@mednet.ucla.edu
.
We allow limited equipment rentals of our low fidelity simulators, task trainers, virtual simulators, and clinical equipment to residents. See our
Resources
page for more details.
