Nursing Students
Nursing Student Simulation Training
The UCLA Simulation Center partners with the
UCLA School of Nursing
to provide simulation education to nursing students at our Rosenfeld Hall facility.
Courses for Nursing Students
While a number of courses are held at the School of Nursing Simulation Lab in the Factor Building, training courses offered for nursing students at the UCLA Simulation Center include:
Nursing Prelicensure Physical Assessment Lab
Advanced Medical Surgical Sim
Procedure Lab
Ventilator Training
Newborn Assessment
Heart Sounds Workshop
ICU Skills
Emerging Infectious Disease Sim
Resources for Nursing Students
If you are a nursing student interested in exploring simulation training, reserving space in Rosenfeld Hall, conducting research that involves simulation, or becoming a simulation instructor, please reach out to us by email at
sim@mednet.ucla.edu
.
We allow limited equipment rentals of our low fidelity simulators, task trainers, virtual simulators, and clinical equipment to nursing students. See our
Resources
page for more details.
