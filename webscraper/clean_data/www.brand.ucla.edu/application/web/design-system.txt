    <div id="usability" class="page-content">
      <div class="page-section">

        <h2>Why use a design system?</h2>

        <p>A design system serves as a single source of truth for design and development teams, providing them with a common language and a shared set of tools. This provides benefits to teams as outlined in &ldquo;Design Systems 101&rdquo; by Therese Fessenden, Nielsen Norman Group.</p>

        <ul class="unordered-list">
          <li>Design and development can be created quickly and at scale.</li>
          <li>It frees design resources to focus on larger, more complex problems.</li>
          <li>It creates a unified language within and between crossfunctional teams.</li>
          <li>It creates visual consistency across products, channels and siloed departments.</li>
          <li>It can help educate junior designers and content contributors.</li>
        </ul>

        <p class="mt-80">
          <a class="callout-link" href="https://designsystem.brand.ucla.edu/">designsystem.brand.ucla.edu</a>
        </p>
