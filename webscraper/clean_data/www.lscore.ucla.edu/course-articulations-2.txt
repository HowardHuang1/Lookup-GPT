How to Submit a Course Equivalency Request
If an articulation does not exist, you may submit the below Equivalency Request form.
To submit the request, fill out the Equivalency Request form below and attach your course syllabus and transcripts.
All requests will be reviewed within 3-5 weeks. We will contact you by email once a decision has been made by the faculty committee.
