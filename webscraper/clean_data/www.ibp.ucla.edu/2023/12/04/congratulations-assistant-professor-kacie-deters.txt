Congratulations to Assistant Professor Kacie Deters for being the recipient of the 2023 Turken Research Award!
Sam and Ida Turken Charitable Foundation
The directors of the Sam and Ida Turken Charitable Foundation along with its president, Beth Devermont the daughter of Phyllis Turken Shamberg who began donating funds to Alzheimer’s research on behalf of the Sam and Ida Turken Charitable Foundation in the 1990’s, continue her mother’s legacy by providing an annual gift to support early career researchers at UCLA who are committed to studying Alzheimer’s disease.
