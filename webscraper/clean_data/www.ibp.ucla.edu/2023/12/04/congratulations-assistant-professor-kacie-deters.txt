<p>Congratulations to Assistant Professor Kacie Deters for being the recipient of the 2023 Turken Research Award!</p>



<p><strong>Sam and Ida Turken Charitable Foundation</strong></p>



<p>The directors of the Sam and Ida Turken Charitable Foundation along with its president, Beth Devermont the daughter of Phyllis Turken Shamberg who began donating funds to Alzheimers research on behalf of the Sam and Ida Turken Charitable Foundation in the 1990s, continue her mothers legacy by providing an annual gift to support early career researchers at UCLA who are committed to studying Alzheimers disease.</p>
