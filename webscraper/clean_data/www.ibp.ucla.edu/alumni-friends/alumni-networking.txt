Why BruinWorks?
BruinWorks is the online, interactive network exclusively for UCLA alumni. This dynamic site allows alumni to connect professionally and personally. BruinWorks is a UCLA graduate’s most valuable resource.
BruinWorks enables you to:
Search for jobs, résumés and alumni businesses
Network socially and professionally in one place
Access the only comprehensive alumni directory available
Explore an all-inclusive listing of UCLA events worldwide
Connect your Bruin experience to other online networks
Find reliable content from university resources
Please visit
www.bruinworks.com
and click on “LOG IN” in the top right corner. If you don’t have an account, you can click on “SIGN UP”. If you do have an account, you can log-in with your e-mail address and password.
