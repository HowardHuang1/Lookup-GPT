Astronomy Live!
Welcome!
Astronomy Live! is UCLA's award-winning Astronomy outreach program run by a group of graduate students in the Department of Physics 
and Astronomy. We are developing a program that visits schools and local events to interact with the community in addition to fostering 
interest in astronomy and science in general.
