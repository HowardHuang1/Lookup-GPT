UCLA Computer Science Affiliates Program (CSAP)
Mission Statement
The
UCLA Computer Science Affiliates Program (CSAP)
is a gateway for collaboration between UCLA Computer Science and the industry member companies can engage in all three aspects of UCLA’s mission: connecting students with the next step beyond school, educating the students, and introducing innovative research results. This program also gives exclusive recruiting access to member companies.
Funds obtained through the CSAP are primarily used for:
Funding our award-winning student chapters ACM and UPE
Sponsorship for sending promising female students to the Grace Hopper Conference
Funding of the Outstanding Student Awards each year
Affiliate Memberships
