				<h5 class="et_pb_toggle_title">CalFresh Initiative</h5>
				<div class="et_pb_toggle_content clearfix"><div>
<p><a href="https://www.getcalfresh.org/?source=ucla">CalFresh</a> is California&#8217;s version of SNAP (the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program), a federally-funded food assistance program, colloquially known as &#8220;food stamps.&#8221; Many students qualify for CalFresh and can receive up to $250/month to spend on groceries at places like Ralphs, Trader Joe&#8217;s, Whole Foods, Target, and participating Farmers Markets. Once enrolled, participants will receive an EBT (Electronic Benefits Transfer) card in the mail, which is reloaded monthly and functions as a debit card at participating grocery stores.</p>
<p>For more information/ to see if you qualify please visit: <a href="https://basicneeds.ucla.edu/services/calfresh">https://basicneeds.ucla.edu/services/calfresh</a></p>

<p>If you are planning to do the thesis option, please be sure to submit your <a href="https://grad.ucla.edu/gasaa/library/masnomin.pdf"><strong>Nomination of Committee Form.</strong> </a>As mentioned at the CS MS Orientation as well as in the CS Grad Student Handbook, it is the student&#8217;s responsibility to find and obtain the faculty members consent to being on your thesis committee before turning this form in  (advisor/chair must be CS faculty, one other CS faculty must serve on the committee and the third can be a faculty outside the CS department) along with your ATC packet by <strong><u> January 19, 2024</u> You do not need to obtain the Departmental Chair/Authorized Faculty Departmental Advisor&#8217;s signature on the Nomination of MS Committee Form in order to turn it in along with your ATC packet (as long as you have received the consent of your advisor and committee via email that they agree to serve on your thesis committee that will suffice).  If you are doing the thesis route, please be sure to attend an ETD workshop. For information on filing your thesis electronically and the date(s) for the ETD workshop please visit: </strong><a href="https://grad.ucla.edu/academics/calendar/thesis-dissertation-filing-deadlines-and-workshops/"><strong>https://grad.ucla.edu/academics/calendar/thesis-dissertation-filing-deadlines-and-workshops/</strong></a><strong>,</strong></p>
<p>If you are doing an MS thesis you must upload your thesis and have your committee sign off on it electronically <strong>by Friday, March 22, 2024.</strong></p>
<p><strong>Finishing the Capstone/Thesis This Quarter</strong></p>
<p><em>Capstone Route</em></p>
<p>If you are planning to submit your Capstone this quarter, the  deadline to submit the <strong><a href="https://www.cs.ucla.edu/wp-content/uploads/cs/Capstone-Results-form-updated-Jan-2021.pdf">Capstone Results Form</a></strong> with all the advisor and committee signatures and abstract via email to jalvarez@cs.ucla.edu is <strong><u>Friday, March 22, 2024.</u></strong></p>
<p>An example of the Capstone Results Form packet can be found <a href="https://www.cs.ucla.edu/wp-content/uploads/cs/Capstone-Project-Packet-Example.pdf"><strong>HERE</strong></a>.</p>
<p>You should have already advanced to candidacy or be submitting the advancement to candidacy packet this quarter (If you are submitting the advancement to candidacy packet this quarter you must be registered and enrolled). Reminder you cannot advance to candidacy and be on filing fee the same quarter; you must submit your advancement to candidacy packet one quarter prior to the quarter you plan to be on filing fee.</p>
<p>As mentioned at the CS MS Orientation as well as in the <strong><a href="https://www.cs.ucla.edu/graduate-student-handbook/">CS Grad Student Handbook</a></strong>, it is the student&#8217;s responsibility to find their capstone/thesis advisor (must be CS faculty and make sure to have them sign off on the Advisor Consent Form and email it to jalvarez@cs.ucla.edu) along with one other CS faculty to be on the committee and another faculty member (this faculty member may be outside the CS Department or also could be CS faculty) to review and sign off on their capstone.</p>
<p>The capstone advisor and committee members may sign off via email but the student must consolidate this into one pdf where the capstone results form is in front (under signature write &#8211; see email), a copy of your abstract (one paragraph long will suffice) and include the email thread where the faculty signed off. They may also have the faculty cc&#8217; jalvarez@cs.ucla.edu when they sign off on their capstone via email.</p>
<p>The quarter that you are submitting your Capstone or Thesis you must either be registered and enrolled full-time (for Summer you must be enrolled in a minimum of 4 units) or have submitted an <a href="https://grad.ucla.edu/academics/graduate-study/filing-fee-application/">electronic filing fee application</a> by the deadline provided that you meet the qualifications to submit a Filing Fee application.</p>
<p>Note, if you are finishing in Summer please get your committee and advisor&#8217;s approval in Spring Quarter that they will be available to review/approve your thesis/capstone over the Summer.</p></div>

				<div class="et_pb_toggle_content clearfix"><p><strong>Filing Fee </strong></p>
<p><strong>Filing fee means that you are about to finish all your course work for the Breadth Requirement as well as the Program of Study by this quarter and need an additional quarter to finish your capstone/thesis (you’re welcome to finish it all the same quarter they finish all their course requirements but some students need extra time). “Filing Fee” for the next quarter (i.e. you have finished taking all the Program of Study/Breadth Req courses and Advance to Candidacy in Winter 2023 and will go on Filing Fee in Spring 2023 to work on the capstone/thesis that you will submit in Winter 2023) as all you need to do is work on and submit your capstone/thesis (you cannot be enrolled in courses when you are on filing fee).</strong></p>
<p>If you are unsure that you will be able to finish your capstone/MS program and might need more time, <strong>we strongly recommend students register and enroll and not submit an application for filing fee</strong>. If you need more time after filing fee you will need to apply for readmission back into the MS program which is laborious and takes time for both the student and various UCLA offices involved.</p>
<p><strong>Filing Fee is not required to do and is completely optional</strong> provided that you meet the requirements (i.e. Advance to Candidacy one quarter prior to the quarter you plan on being on Filing Fee) and submit your application by the deadline. Please note the filing fee application is separate from the Advancement to Candidacy petition form.</p>

If you are in UCLA Housing, please notify UCLA Housing that you will apply for filing fee and ask if it will impact your Housing or not. For a list of services suspended/impacted while on filing fee please visit the <a href="https://grad.ucla.edu/academics/graduate-study/filing-fee-application/">filing fee website</a>.</p>
