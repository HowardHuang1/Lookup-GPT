Our Mission
The Controller’s Office resides within Business & Finance Solutions, a division of the Office of the Vice Chancellor & Chief Financial Officer. We are committed to providing a wide range of business, financial, accounting, and reporting services in support of UCLA’s mission of education, research, and public service. We serve with the highest ethical standards in order to maintain strong internal controls and safeguard our University assets, while fostering a work environment which embraces teamwork and continuous improvement. Our departments strive to deliver excellent customer service built on collaborative stakeholder partnerships to promote an environment of shared financial accountability.
