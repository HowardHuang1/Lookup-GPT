Treasury
UCLA Treasury Services is instrumental in the facilitation of financial and operational transactions on behalf of UCLA. In addition, Treasury Services manages UCLA’s banking relationships and operates the UCLA Internal Banking Center for the facilitation of UCLA internal loans. In collaboration with campus departments and UCOP teams, Treasury Services oversees the optimization of University treasury policies and practices while maintaining a strong control environment.
