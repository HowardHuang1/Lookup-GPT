FAQs
How can I apply online?
Available 24/7 for Students & Career.
Visit our Jobs page (you’re here!) and select the job you’re interested in.
Fill out the application and submit.
What if I’m a UCLA Extension student?
Only those UCLA Extension Students that are concurrently enrolled at UCLA are eligible to apply.
Please note that UCLA Extension courses are separate from UCLA courses. Any UCLA Extension Students wishing to apply must also be enrolled in university courses offered through UCLA.
Incoming freshman/transfer students
If you don’t have your BruinCard yet, please bring your acceptance letter and a photo ID when you are ready to apply.
