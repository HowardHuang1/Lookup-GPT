ASUCLA STUDENT JOB FAIR
Associated Students UCLA operates all of the on-campus restaurants and stores with over 1400 student employees. We need baristas, cashiers, office staff, even graphic designers and computer experts. Whether you have never worked a day in your life or you want to bring your special skills to the job, ASUCLA needs you!

How do I apply for a job?
We will hold our annual ASUCLA Job Fair during Welcome Week in Bruin Plaza. But if you want to get a jump, you can apply online. Start checking for new postings after Labor Day!
Apply @ ASUCLA
Advantages of working for ASUCLA
On-campus jobs with flexible schedules to work around classes and exams.
A 20% Employee Discount on BearWear and lots of other items at the UCLA Store. Restaurant employees also get a Meal Allowance on work days.
Opportunities for rapid promotion to supervisory positions. This means you will have the chance to earn more and to build your resume with on-the-job training.
We hire Work Study students!
Work in a dynamic, student-centered atmosphere. Your co-workers, customers and supervisors will be UCLA students, just like you.
