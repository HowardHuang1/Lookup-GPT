Who are we?
Established in concert with UCLA in 1919, Associated Students UCLA (ASUCLA) is a nonprofit organization composed of four entities —
Undergraduate Students Association Council
(USAC),
Graduate Students Association
(GSA),
Student Media
and Student-Run Enterprises — that provide excellent services, products and facilities for the entire UCLA community. ASUCLA is governed by a
board of directors
with a student majority-appointment and an executive director who serves ex-officio.
