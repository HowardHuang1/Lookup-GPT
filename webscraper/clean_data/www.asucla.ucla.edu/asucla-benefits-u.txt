Join ASUCLA Benefits U
As a UCLA student, you’re already a member of one of the largest and strongest student unions in the nation – the Associated Students UCLA, also called ASUCLA. Now you can get even more for being part of ASUCLA from ASUCLA Benefits U!
It’s easy, and best of all, it’s FREE! All full-time regularly-enrolled UCLA students are automatically members and will receive emails with coupons and other promotional information.
WHAT YOU GET FROM ASUCLA BENEFITS U
Monthly Coupons
— get coupons for great deals and discounts at UCLA Restaurants!
Advance Notice
— find out about upcoming events before anyone else…like UCLA Store sales, job fairs, and more!
Everyday Discounts
— get exclusive ASUCLA Benefits U discounts at the UCLA Store!
