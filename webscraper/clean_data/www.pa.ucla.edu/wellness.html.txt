Mission statement
“Physics & Astronomy Wellness aims to foster a healthy environment for all members of the department and build a community that prioritizes work-life balance. Our mission is to cultivate and maintain supportive relationships among undergraduates, grads, faculty, staff, scientists, and postdocs, by assessing department climate and creating wellness centered events.”
