Resources and Organizations for Graduate Students
CAPS
Counseling and Psychological Services
CAPS supports your mental health needs as you pursue your academic goals.

The Ashe Center
Health and Wellness Services
The center is devoted to providing quality, accessible, state-of-the-art health care and wellness education to support the holistic development of UCLA students. Please see
The Ashe Center official website
for updated UCSHIP pricing and any modifications for the current plan year, including this year’s waiver deadline. Similarly, please refer to the
UCLA Immunization Requirement website
for updated FAQs, deadlines, and more.
Video Link 01
UCLA Career Center
Master’s, Ph.D., and Postdoctoral Career Services
The center helps prepare for your future career with one-on-one appointments, workshops, networking events and more, designed specifically for advanced-degree students.
Video Link 01
Graduate Writing Center
UCLA Graduate Writing Center
The center offers a variety of workshops and programs throughout the year. During the summer, we offer various dissertation “boot camps” for graduate students working on dissertation proposals and dissertations.
Video Link 01
Graduate Student Resource Center
UCLA Graduate Student Resource Center
The GSRC works to form partnerships and other collaborative relationships on campus to connect students with resources and services, and develops programs and workshops to address unmet needs that help graduate students reach their academic and professional goals.
Video Link 01

UCLA Technology Development Group
UCLA Technology Development Group

eGSA
The Engineering Graduate Students Association of UCLA
The association represents the graduate students of the seven departments of the Henry Samueli School of Engineering and Applied Science. For more information on how to get involved, please see the
eGSA website
.

GradSWE
Graduate Society of Women Engineers at UCLA
GradSWE serves to support, inspire, and empower the current and future female engineering community to excel in all endeavors. For more information on how to get involved, please see the
GradSWE website
.
