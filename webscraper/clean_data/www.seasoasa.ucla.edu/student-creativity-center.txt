Our School’s ‘Technological Sandbox’, a place for our community to create, design, disaRenessemble, and re-engineer devices and gadgets of their own imagination.

Academic year – Student Design Organizations engaged in design activity.
Summer – Engineering Science Corps Outreach programs.
