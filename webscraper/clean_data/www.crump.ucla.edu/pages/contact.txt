Directions
The UCLA campus is 12 miles North on the 405 freeway from Los Angeles International Airport (LAX), and a 25 minute drive in minimum traffic. If you are only in Los Angeles on UCLA business, it may be most convenient to take a taxi from the airport; the fare is about $50.
Heading either North or South on the 405 Freeway exit Wilshire Blvd East. Turn left at Westwood Blvd and continue to the parking kiosk between Parking structures 8 and 9, just North of Charles E. Young Drive South. The current daily parking rate is $12 per vehicle and other parking options may be found
here
If parking in Lot 9, the rooftop level is shared with the entrance to the CNSI Building, where the Crump Institute is housed.
