<div style='padding-bottom:10px; ' class='av-special-heading av-special-heading-h1  blockquote modern-quote  avia-builder-el-2  avia-builder-el-no-sibling   '><h1 class='av-special-heading-tag '  itemprop="headline"  ><span>The UCLA College of Letters and Science  <br /> Reflecting on the UCLA Experience</span></h1><div class='special-heading-border'><div class='special-heading-inner-border' ></div></div></div>

<section class="av_textblock_section "  itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="https://schema.org/CreativeWork" ><div class='avia_textblock  '   itemprop="text" ><p>In Spring 2023, all seniors in the UCLA College of Letters and Science were asked to complete a survey on their experiences at the university. The survey explored students’ views regarding their studies, lives on campus, and plans after graduation. Approximately 6,070 College seniors responded to the survey, representing approximately 82 percent of the graduating class.</p>
<p>This website features some of the main results of the survey.</p>
<p>Our goal is to ensure that the findings of this senior survey, and those that will follow, will help shape the UCLA experience for many generations of Bruins.</p>
<p>Adriana Galván<br />
Dean of Undergraduate Education</p>
