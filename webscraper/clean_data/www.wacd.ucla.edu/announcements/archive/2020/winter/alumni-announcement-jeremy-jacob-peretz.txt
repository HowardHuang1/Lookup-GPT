Alumni Announcement: Jeremy Jacob Peretz
WAC/D alum Jeremy Peretz's film,
Just 3 People Talking,
will screen later this month to kick off the Timehri Film Festival at the Castellani House National Art Gallery in Georgetown, Guyana. The opening night programme, titled "
The Ones Who Shared Their Souls
," will bring together "
The Procession
, from acclaimed contemporary Guyanese-British artist, Hew Locke," with one of Jeremy's filmic collaborations with Joan Cambridge-Mayfield. A related collaborative project of Joan and Jeremy's--
Jumbi Justice
--is also featured in the current issue of
Anthropology News
.
For more information, including how to view the film projects, follow links provided below.
