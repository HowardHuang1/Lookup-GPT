ALUMNI ANNOUNCEMENT - Brynn Shiovitz
Brynn Shiovitz holds a Ph.D. from UCLA’s Culture and Performance Program in the World Arts & Cultures Department. Her new book, Behind the Screen, subtitled Tap Dance, Race, and Invisibility During Hollywood’s Golden Age is reviewed in the most recently Village Voice.
