Brisa Smith Flores has earned the Terra Foundation Postdoctoral Fellowship at the Smithsonian American Art Museum
Brisa Smith Flores has earned the Terra Foundation Postdoctoral Fellowship at the Smithsonian American Art Museum. She will examine transnational Afro-diasporic relations within photography through a project titled, "Picturing Diaspora: Tony Gleaton and Familiarity in Afro-Latin Portraiture." She has also earned a Chancellor's Postdoctoral Fellowship at the University of California, Irvine. Under the mentorship of professor Bridget R. Cooks, she will expand on her dissertation work during her tenure at UCI.
