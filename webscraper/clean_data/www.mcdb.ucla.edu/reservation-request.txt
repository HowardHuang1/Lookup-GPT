Molecular, Cell and Developmental Biology Research Conference
Annenberg Community Beach House
Friday, December 8, 2023 and Saturday, December 9, 2023
a
CONFERENCE REGISTRATION
Complete a form for each individual attending the conference.
Conference start time is Friday, December 8th at 1:00 P.M. until 10:00 P.M. and the conference will continue Saturday, December 9th from 8:00 A.M. until 4:00 P.M.
*kickoff mixer and lunch start time is Friday, December 8th at 11 A.M.
There is
no fee
for conference registration, however space is limited for those wishing to attend in-person. If the number of attendees exceeds the conference capacity, a waitlist will be created and priority given to in-person poster presenters, postdocs and graduate students. If your plans change and you can no longer attend in person, please let us know immediately to give other attendees the opportunity to attend.
Registration is now closed.
