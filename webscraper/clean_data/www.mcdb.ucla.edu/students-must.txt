Undergraduate Research – MCDB 199A-D :  Students Must
Students Must:
Be majors in Molecular, Cell & Developmental Biology.
Have successfully completed Life Science 3, 23L, and 4 OR LS7A, 7B, 7C, 23L, and 107 and meet the GPA requirements stated below.
Be either a junior or senior with an overall GPA of 3.0 or higher, and 3.0 or higher GPA in the major coursework by the time of application.
SRP 99 is NOT a pre-requisite for MCDB 199A-D.
Have completed lab safety training (see
Laboratory Safety Training
).
Read and follow all guidelines detailed on this website under MCDB 199A-B, C, D.  For any questions, consult the MCDB Undergraduate Counselor (128 Hershey Hall).
Not be working for pay in the sponsor’s lab at the same time you are enrolled for academic credit in MCDB 199A-D courses.
Students may not enroll in a 199A-D course if they have outstanding Incompletes in a previous 196, 199, or 198A-B, C, D course.
Transfer students may not enroll during their first quarter at UCLA.
