Undergraduate Advising
Current MCDB Majors:
30-Minute Advising Appointments
Schedule a 30-minute remote or in-person advising appointment with an MCDB undergraduate advisor via
MyUCLA
.
Remote appointments are available on Mondays, Tuesdays, Wednesdays, Thursdays, and Fridays. In-person appointments are available on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Thursdays.
Drop-In Advising
Have a quick question? The MCDB undergraduate advisors are available to meet with students for short sessions (5-10 minutes) during our regular
drop-in advising hours:
Wednesdays:  9:00 AM – 12:00 PM via
Zoom
(
You will enter a waiting room and be admitted when an advisor is available).
Thursdays:  1:00 PM – 4:00 PM in-person in 128 Hershey Hall
Non-MCDB Majors:
Send an email to
undergradmcdb@lifesci.ucla.edu
to schedule an advising appointment with an MCDB undergraduate advisor.
