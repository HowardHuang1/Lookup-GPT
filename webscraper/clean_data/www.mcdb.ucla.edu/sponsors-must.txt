Undergraduate Research – MCDB 199A-D  :  Sponsors Must
Sponsors Must:
be either faculty members in the Department of Molecular, Cell, and Developmental Biology;
or be listed on the
Eligible Faculty Research Sponsors for MCDB Majors list
.
This list is available in the MCDB Undergraduate Office located at 128 Hershey Hall.   Only MCDB majors may enroll in MCDB 199A-D with a non-MCDB faculty member on the approved list.
Note:
Students working with non-MCDB faculty members who are on the list of approved faculty research sponsors must have their sponsor complete the
Outside Sponsor Agreement
and submit it with a timeline for each quarter of their project, along with their research proposal and other application materials (see
How to Apply and Enroll
).
