U See LA Optometry
Comprehensive eye exams, eyeglasses, sunglasses, and contact lenses.
U See LA Optometry is now open for eye exam, glasses and contact lens services by appointment only. We have adopted all sterilization and safety practices recommended by the CDC, UCLA and the Ashe Student Health center to ensure maximum protection for our patients and staff.
Appointments must be made by calling
310-267-4772
between the hours of 8am to 5pm Monday through Friday. Please leave a message if you reach our office recording, we will return your call as soon as possible.
Contact lens orders with valid prescriptions can be filled through email requests at
optometry@ashe.ucla.edu
or by calling us at the above number.
