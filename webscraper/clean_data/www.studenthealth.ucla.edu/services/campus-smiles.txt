Campus Smiles Dental at UCLA
Comprehensive dentistry conveniently located at your student health center. Appointments are available now.
Campus Smiles Dental Office is located on the second floor of The Arthur Ashe Student Health & Wellness Center and features comprehensive dentistry services for the UCLA Bruin community. Dental Services at The Ashe Center began in the fall of 2019 and has proven to be a popular and convenient healthcare amenity for UCLA students.
Most dental services are covered with UCSHIP Delta Dental PPO. The Campus Smiles Dental Office at UCLA accepts most all dental insurance plans including the UCSHIP Delta Dental Plan. Consult your UCSHIP Explanation of Benefits or The Ashe Center insurance office for exclusions.
