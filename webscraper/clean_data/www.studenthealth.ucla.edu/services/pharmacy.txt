    node.innerHTML = '';
    var toc = document.createElement('div');
    toc.classList.add('table-of-contents');
    node.parentNode.insertBefore(toc, node);
    console.log(node.parentNode.parentNode);
    var activeCategory = '';
    for(var i=0; i<data.length; i++) {
        var item = data[i];
        if(activeCategory != item.category) {
            var h2 = document.createElement('h2');
            h2.innerHTML = item.category;
            activeCategory = `${item.category}`;
            node.appendChild(h2);
        }
        var h3 = document.createElement('h3');
        h3.innerHTML = item.question;

                a.setAttribute('href', `https:///www.uclahealth.org/${a.getAttribute('href')}`);
            }
        }

        node.appendChild(h3);
        node.appendChild(div);
    }

  <div class="body-content paragraph--section" data-section-title="Bruin Health Pharmacy FAQs">
          <h2>
Bruin Health Pharmacy FAQs
</h2>
      
<h3>Who can use Bruin Health Pharmacy?</h3>

<p>Students enrolled at any UC campus and any UC faculty or staff member have access to Bruin Health Pharmacy services.</p>

<h3>Where is Bruin Health Pharmacy located?</h3>

<p>Bruin Health Pharmacy is located at 308 Westwood Plaza A-250 located in Ackerman Union, A-Level. You can access Bruin Health Pharmacy via the main stairway at Ackerman Union.</p>

<h3>Can someone else check-in/ pick up prescriptions for me?</h3>

<p>Yes. You may designate another individual, a PROXY, to pick up your prescription. Please notify the pharmacy in advance. Your PROXY must have their own photo ID, be over 18-years-old, and know your ID number.</p>

<h3>What forms of payment are accepted?</h3>

<p>For students, all charges will be applied to your Bruin Bill account within 7 to 10 business days. For faculty and staff members, we accept all major credit cards as well as Apple, Google, and Samsung Pay.</p>

<h3>I just had an appointment. Is my prescription ready?</h3>

<p>Please allow one hour for Bruin Health Pharmacy to process your prescription(s). If you have opted-in for text messages with Bruin Health Pharmacy, you will be notified once your prescription is ready.</p>

<h3>Do you accept any returns on prescriptions?</h3>

<p>No, once a medication leaves the Pharmacy it cannot be returned or exchanged.</p>

<h3>What types of insurance plans are accepted?</h3>

<p>For UC students, we accept UC SHIP and for UC staff or faculty, we accept UC Care and Health Net Blue &amp; Gold.</p>

<h3>Can I pick up more than a 30-day supply of medication?</h3>

<p>Yes, depending on your insurance coverage period, and the original written quantity of the prescription. &nbsp;</p>

<h3>How do I know if I have refills remaining on my medications?</h3>

<p>The lower left hand corner of your prescription label will indicate the balance remaining on your prescription.</p>

<h3>How do I request prescription refills?</h3>

<p>Go to <a href="http://www.studenthealth.ucla.edu">www.studenthealth.ucla.edu</a>, log in to the Patient Portal, click on ‘Messages,’ click on ‘New Message,’ then fill out your request. You may also make a refill request in-person or over the phone.</p>

<h3>Can I refill a prescription from another pharmacy at Bruin Health Pharmacy?</h3>

<p>Yes. We may transfer your prescription into Bruin Health Pharmacy. Provide us with the pharmacy name, phone number, medication name, and prescription number (if available). Transfers may take up to two business days to complete.&nbsp;</p>

<h3>What is your refill policy?</h3>

<p>Please allow 1 to 2 business days for refills to be completed. Non-controlled substance refills are generally available 23 days after the previous fill; controlled substance refills are generally available 27 days after the previous fill.</p>
