Text Alerts from The Ashe Center
Did you know?
The Ashe Center can send you text message alerts about your upcoming appointments, prescriptions, and more. Opt-in for text messages today.
How to Opt-In to Text Messages from The Ashe Center
Log into The Ashe Center Patient Portal using your Bruin Online ID #
Click Profile on the menu located to the far left of the screen
On the Profile page, navigate to the Text Messaging option and click Edit
Under Edit Mobile Phone, verify your phone number is correct
Click the checkbox: "I would like to receive text messages."
Make sure to select your Mobile Carrier (AT&T, Verizon, etc.)
Click Continue to review your submission
Click Confirm to complete your mobile registration
How to Opt-In to Text Messages from Bruin Health Pharmacy
Visit or call Bruin Health Pharmacy at
