Psychology Advising Services
Psychology Department Advisors are available to support current UCLA undergraduates through in-person drop-in advising hours. Our office is located in 1530 Pritzker Hall, and is open from 9:00-11:30am and 1:00-4:00pm, Monday-Thursday for general inquiries. Note our drop-in hours below. Hours may vary due to staff trainings, events, and holidays. Please check this webpage for updates to our drop-in hours. Psychology Advising staff work remotely on Fridays.

Drop-in Advising
Drop-in Advising is offered in 1530 Pritzker Hall, on the following days/times:
Mondays: 1:00-3:00pm
Tuesdays, Wednesdays, Thursdays: 9:00-11:00am & 1:00-3:00pm
*Please be sure to bring a picture ID to meet with an advisor.
Drop-In Advising Instructions
Students should attend drop-in hours for assistance with any of the following topics:
Major and pre-major questions (Psychology, Psychobiology, Cognitive Science)
Applied Developmental Psychology minor course requirements
Short-term course planning for major courses
Enrollment questions/concerns about Psychology courses
Questions about Psychology department fieldwork/research opportunities
When you arrive for drop-in advising, intake staff will record your questions and place you on the waitlist to see an advisor. Wait times vary, and arriving early during drop-in advising shifts increases your chances of a shorter wait. Please be aware that during busy times, like enrollment, drop-in appointments fill up quickly, and you may need to return the next day due to high demand.
