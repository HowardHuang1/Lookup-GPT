Location and Map Information
Located on the main campus of UCLA, the Psychology complex spans three interconnected buildings: Pritzker Hall, Psychology Building and Franz Hall.
Parking
The closest parking structure to our complex is Parking Lot 2. Lot 2 is located at the Westholme/Hilgard entrance to campus. Our complex is directly across the street from Lot 2.
Parking is available through automated
self-service pay stations in
Lot 2. Hourly rates range from $3(1 hour)-$14(all-day) in this parking lot.
If guest parking is arranged prior to your visit, you will be given a coupon code and instructions from your department host prior to arriving on campus.
