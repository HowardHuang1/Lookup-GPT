How to Change Your Password
Please read our guideline on
How to Create Strong Passwords
first.
The password must conform to the following requirements:
Be 8 characters in length
Have at least 1 letter and 1 number
Cannot be a full dictionary word (hackers can use a list of dictionary words to break passwords)
CHANGING YOUR PASSWORD
(For TA accounts, email help@seas.ucla.edu to change the password for you.)
Go to
https://my.engineering.ucla.edu
and log in (at top-right)
In “My Profile” block, click “Change SEASnet Password”
You can view your username and/or change your password (wait 5-10 minutes before trying to use your new password)
FORGOTTEN USERNAME and/or PASSWORD
You can request to get your username and/or reset your password using one of the following options
(due to the closure of the Help Desk during this time, only Option 1 is available):
Option 1
– Online, see the instructions for “CHANGING YOUR PASSWORD” above
Option 2
– By coming to the SEASnet Help Desk (2684 BH) with your UCLA BruinCard (or other Picture ID) during normal business hours:
Monday – 10AM – NOON / 1PM – 4:30
Tue-Fri –    8AM – NOON / 1PM – 4:30
We are closed from NOON – 1PM for lunch
Once your identify is verified we can assist you in changing your password.
