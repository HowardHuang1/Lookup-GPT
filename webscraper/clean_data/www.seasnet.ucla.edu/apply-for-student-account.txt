Instructions for applying for a SEASnet students account
Please first make sure that you have satisfied the requirement to apply for account at
https://www.seasnet.ucla.edu/seasnet-accounts/
Once you have satisfied those requirement, you can use the following steps to apply for account:
Go to
https://my.engineering.ucla.edu
Click “Log in” at top-right and login with your UCLA Logon ID
In the “My Profile” box on the left, click “SEASnet Student Account Application”
