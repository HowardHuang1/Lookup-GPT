Welcome to the IT Support Center, focusing the efforts of support teams within IT Services so you can easily get the assistance you need.
How to Contact Us
Email or Call
Email us at
help@it.ucla.edu
or call (310)
267-HELP
(4357). Support analysts will take down the details and route your question or issue to the applicable IT Services group.
Please note: We now provide
24/7 phone support
for UCLA Logon ID lookups and password resets, multi-factor authentication (MFA)/Duo assistance, and for access assistance to on-call staff for operational emergencies.
Visit Us
The IT Support Center is located on campus at 124 Kerckhoff Hall.
Winter Quarter 2024 front desk walk-in hours:
Monday through Friday: 9:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m.
Please see the
Contact Us
page for holiday hours and closures.
