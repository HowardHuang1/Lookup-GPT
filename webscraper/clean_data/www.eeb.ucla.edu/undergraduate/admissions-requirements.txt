Admissions Requirements
For admissions requirements, visit the website for Undergraduate Admissions and Relations with Schools (UARS):
http:// www.admissions.ucla.edu/infoprospective.htm
. Please be aware that the department has no involvement in undergraduate admissions decisions. All inquiries about admissions should be directed to UARS.
FRESHMAN APPLICANTS
If you plan to apply as a first-year college student, visit
http://www.admissions.ucla.edu/Prospect/Adm_fr.htm
for admissions criteria.
TRANSFER APPLICANTS
If you plan to apply as a transfer student, visit
http://www.admissions.ucla.edu/prospect/adm_tr.htm
for admissions criteria.
Students who wish to apply to a major in the Division of Life Sciences as a TRANSFER student must complete the following prior to matriculation at UCLA:
Required
One (1) year of General Biology with Lab*
*Preferably equivalent to Life Sciences 1 and Life Sciences 2 at UCLA.
One (1) year of General Chemistry with Lab
One (1) year of Calculus
One (1) semester of Organic Chemistry with Lab
Recommended
Second semester of Organic Chemistry with Lab
One (1) year of Calculus-based Physics
TRANSFER APPLICANTS, FALL 2008 AND LATER:
Transfer students who are not admitted to UCLA as a major in the Division of Life Sciences may not change to a Life Sciences major upon matriculation to UCLA.
You may view additional admissions requirements here:
https://admission.ucla.edu/apply/transfer/deciding-on-major/major-preparation-the-college/requirements-for-life-sciences-majors
