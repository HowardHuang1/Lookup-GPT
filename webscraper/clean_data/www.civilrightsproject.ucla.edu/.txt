The Civil Rights Project at UCLA
The mission of the
Civil Rights Project/Proyecto Derechos Civiles
is to help renew the civil rights movement by bridging the worlds of ideas and action, to be a preeminent source of intellectual capital within that movement, and to deepen the understanding of the issues that must be resolved to achieve racial and ethnic equity as society moves through the great transformation of the 21st century.
