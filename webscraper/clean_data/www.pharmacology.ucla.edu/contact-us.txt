Directions
The Pharmacology Department main administrative offices are located on the 2nd floor of the Center for Health Sciences (CHS) 23-145 at 650 Charles E. Young Drive on the UCLA campus in Westwood, California.
From the 405 Freeway, exit Wilshire Blvd. East. Continue east on Wilshire for several blocks. Make a left turn on Westwood Blvd. Make a right at LeConte Ave. then a left at Tiverton into the Center for Health Sciences.
Pay by Space parking is available at the CHS parking structure on Tiverton. Parking permits can also be purchased at the parking kiosk on Westwood Plaza.
