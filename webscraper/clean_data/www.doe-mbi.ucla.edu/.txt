Welcome to the IGP
The UCLA-DOE Institute for Genomics and Proteomics (IGP) comprises research laboratories and core facilities working together on fundamental research and technology developments in several DOE mission areas, including Microbial Systems: Energy and Biomass and Atomic Structure and Enabling Capabilities.

Microbes
Deciphering the metabolism of syntrophic microbial communities and the biogenesis of the cellulosome appendage of the model cellulose degrading microbe, Clostridium thermocellum

Enabling Technologies
Pioneering the new method of micro-electron diffraction (microED); revealing microscale substructure within protein crystals at unprecedented levels of detail

Leadership
The UCLA-DOE IGP brings together the best and brightest researchers from around the world. This inter-disciplinary team of scientists, post doctoral researchers, and graduate students join forces to develop genetic, biological, computational  technologies to accelerate discoveries within the IGP and throughout the broader BER community.
