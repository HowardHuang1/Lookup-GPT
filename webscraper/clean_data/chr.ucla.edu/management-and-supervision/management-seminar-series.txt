Management Seminar Series
These seminars offer unique professional development for University leaders by world-class faculty, right here on campus. They feature relevant tools and approaches that have direct impact on improving organizations, innovations in management education and engage participants and their colleagues in intellectual stimulation and learning.
The theme of the 2023-24 series is
“
Transformative Leadership.
”
It
is designed to help attendees strengthen team leadership skills, leverage core management competencies, enhance professional networks, and influence resources needed to support the University’s commitment to excellence. The series features presentations by Anderson faculty, who are national experts in their fields, and interactive dialogue with campus colleagues.
Quarterly sessions are offered on the following topics:
▪
Leading in a Technology-Driven World
, Terry Kramer
Oct. 11, 2023
▪
Your Future Self
, Hal Hershfield
Nov. 1, 2023
▪
Designing Surveys for Quality Results
, Constanca Esteves-Sorenson
Feb. 7, 2024
▪
How to Effectively Lead Yourself and Others
,
Karl Kuhnert
March 6, 2024
All academic and administrative leaders, faculty, and professional staff at the Career Tracks Manager level 1 and above, Professional 4 or 5, or PSS 5 and equivalent MSP classifications are eligible to attend.
