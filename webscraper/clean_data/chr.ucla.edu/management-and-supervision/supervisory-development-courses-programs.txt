Supervisory Development Courses & Programs
A variety of courses and programs address the needs of UCLA supervisors. They focus on fundamental issues of supervision and help participants to develop practical skills in managing people, as well as introduce them to more sophisticated management theories and techniques. Many courses cover University policies and procedures, which are crucial for successfully dealing with the day-to-day responsibilities required of a supervisor.
