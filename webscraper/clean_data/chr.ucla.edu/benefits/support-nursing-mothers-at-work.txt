Supporting Nursing Mothers at UCLA
UCLA is committed to the health and well-being of its community members, which includes all students, staff and faculty. If you or your partner need support during your pregnancy or accommodations upon returning to work or school, you are encouraged to connect with available campus services. This webpage is intended to provide guidance in finding appropriate resources, as well as understanding what your rights are as a breastfeeding student, faculty or staff member.
