Studying integrative physiology at UCLA is rewarding both professionally and personally. Living in Los Angeles, with its Mediterranean chaparall climate, close access to ocean beaches, coastal mountains, high deserts, and alpine tundra, as well as its thriving urban culture provide the inspiration you need to pursue creative and innovative laboratory research.
