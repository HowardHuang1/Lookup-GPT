MCIP Faculty Administrative Committee
The MCIP Faculty Administrative Committee (FAC) is the program’s leadership and steering committee.  The FAC is composed of eight MCIP faculty members.  The Chair of the FAC is the MCIP Program Chair.  Five members of the committee hold academic appointments in the David Geffen School of Medicine and three members hold academic appointments in the College of Letters & Science—Division of Life Sciences.  The duties of the FAC are the following:
Evaluates applications of faculty who wish to become affiliated with the MCIP PhD program
Reviews admission applications, interviews applicants, and decides who will be admitted into the MCIP PhD program
Advises and meets with first year rotation students
Makes recommendations about changes to the course curriculum and program requirements
Finalizes decisions for the Written Qualifying Exam
Makes decisions and recommendations on student academic cases
