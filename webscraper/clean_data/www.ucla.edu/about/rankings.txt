			<h1>Rankings</h1>

			<p class="p--lead-in p-0">UCLA performs well in all of the national and international rankings of public and private universities, including the most widely known list published by <span class="italic">U.S. News &amp; World Report</span>. Below, you’ll find an overview of some of the more significant and well-known national and international rankings.</p>

		</div>
	</div>
</div>


<div class="section-wrapper">
	<div class="ucla campus">

		<div class="col span_8_of_12">

			<h2 class="pb-24">National Rankings</h2>

			<aside class="stat-wrapper clearfix mb-24">
				<div class="stat-tout">
					<span class="stat-tout__number">#1</span>
					<div class="stat-tout__info-wrap">
						<span class="stat-tout__label">Public University</span>
						<span class="stat-tout__source">U.S. News & World Report, 2023</span>
					</div>
				</div>
			</aside>

			<p class="pb-6">This year, UCLA joins UC Berkeley as the nation’s No. 1 public university, ranking 20th among all national universities in the  <span class="italic">U.S. News & World Report Best Colleges</span> rankings. The <span class="italic">U.S. News & World Report</span> rankings, despite their popularity, tell only part of the story. They place more emphasis on factors that tend to favor private universities, such as endowment size, rate of alumni giving and student-faculty ratios. Nevertheless, they can be a useful tool that focuses largely on factors related to undergraduate education.</p>

			<aside class="stat-wrapper clearfix mb-24">
				<div class="stat-tout">
					<span class="stat-tout__number">#7</span>
					<div class="stat-tout__info-wrap">
						<span class="stat-tout__label">Best Colleges for Veterans Among National Universities</span>
						<span class="stat-tout__source">U.S. News & World Report, 2023</span>
					</div>
				</div>
			</aside>

		</div>

	</div>
</div> <!-- end National Rankings -->



<div class="section-wrapper">
	<div class="ucla campus">

		<div class="col span_8_of_12">

			<h2 class="pb-24">Global Rankings</h2>

			<aside class="stat-wrapper clearfix mb-24">
				<div class="stat-tout">
					<span class="stat-tout__number">#9</span>
					<div class="stat-tout__info-wrap">
						<span class="stat-tout__label">World Reputation Ranking</span>
						<span class="stat-tout__source">Times Higher Education, 2020</span>
					</div>
				</div>
			</aside>

			<p class="pb-6">UCLA ranked 9th out of 100 universities in the <span class="italic">Times Higher Education</span> World Reputation Rankings. Distinct from the annual World University Rankings, these rankings are derived solely from the opinions of senior, published academics from around the globe.</p>

			<aside class="stat-wrapper clearfix mb-24">
				<div class="stat-tout">
					<span class="stat-tout__number">#13</span>
					<div class="stat-tout__info-wrap">
						<span class="stat-tout__label">Global Ranking</span>
						<span class="stat-tout__source">U.S. News & World Report, 2022</span>
					</div>
				</div>
			</aside>

			<p class="pb-6"><span class="italic">U.S. News</span> introduced a new global ranking of universities in 2014. The methodology for this ranking is very different from the publication’s U.S. ranking system. It gives much more weight to academic research and reputation, as well as graduate and professional school quality.</p>

			<aside class="stat-wrapper clearfix mb-24">
				<div class="stat-tout">
					<span class="stat-tout__number">#13</span>
					<div class="stat-tout__info-wrap">
						<span class="stat-tout__label">Academic Ranking of World Universities</span>
						<span class="stat-tout__source">Shanghai Jiao Tong University, 2022</span>
					</div>
				</div>
			</aside>

			<p class="pb-6">More than 1,200 institutions are evaluated in this ranking, which is based heavily on faculty publications and citations, as well as the number of alumni and faculty who have won Nobel Prizes.</p>

			<aside class="stat-wrapper clearfix mb-24">
				<div class="stat-tout">
					<span class="stat-tout__number">#15</span>
					<div class="stat-tout__info-wrap">
						<span class="stat-tout__label">World University Rankings</span>
						<span class="stat-tout__source">Times Higher Education, 2021</span>
					</div>
				</div>
			</aside>

			<p>UCLA was 15th overall among the top 1,102 universities in the world in the respected <span class="italic">Times Higher Education</span> World University Rankings. The rankings rely on performance indicators such as the quality of teaching, research and the international mix of staff and students.</p>

		</div>

	</div>
</div> <!-- end Global Rankings -->



<div class="section-wrapper">
	<div class="ucla campus">

		<div class="col span_8_of_12">

			<h2 class="pb-24">Other Rankings</h2>

			<aside class="stat-wrapper clearfix mb-24">
				<div class="stat-tout">
					<span class="stat-tout__number">#6</span>
					<div class="stat-tout__info-wrap">
						<span class="stat-tout__label">Best Value University</span>
						<span class="stat-tout__source">Forbes Magazine, 2022</span>
					</div>
				</div>
			</aside>

			<p>In 2022 (the most recent ranking), <span class="italic">Forbes</span> evaluates colleges and universities based on alumni earnings, net price, net student debt, school quality, timely graduation and the number of Pell Grant recipients.</p>

			<p class="pb-6"><span class="italic">Forbes</span> revised its methodology in 2018 to put more emphasis on the survey’s three financial factors &mdash; earnings, price and debt &mdash; and the publication switched to net price (which takes into account the impact of students’ financial aid and the costs of room and board) instead of list price.</p>


			<aside class="stat-wrapper clearfix mb-24">
				<div class="stat-tout">
					<span class="stat-tout__number">#9</span>
					<div class="stat-tout__info-wrap">
						<span class="stat-tout__label">Top American Research Universities</span>
						<span class="stat-tout__source">Center for Measuring University Performance, 2019</span>
					</div>
				</div>
			</aside>

			<p>In 2019 (the most recent ranking), the center placed UCLA among the nation’s top research universities &mdash; both public and private. This ranking focuses on nine key measures of university research performance, including competitively awarded research grants and contracts, faculty membership in the National Academies, faculty awards, the number of doctorates awarded and other factors. UCLA was ranked in the top 25 among all research universities in seven of these measures, including:</p>

			<ul class="ucla-list--bulleted">
				<li>7th in the nation in total research expenditures</li>
				<li>8th in the nation in faculty awards</li>
				<li class="pb-6">14th in the nation in national academy memberships</li>
			</ul>
		</div>

	</div>
