Services for the General Public
Learn about how to visit and access our spaces, collections and world-class resources.
We strive to open our collections, spaces, exhibitions and events to as many people as possible! The following are some of the many ways members of the public can visit and access the Library.
Access our Collections
View books and journals in the library's open stack collections, or borrow materials using a UCLA Library Card. Access our rich online collections while physically on campus. Users can log onto the campus wireless network using their personal laptops. Off-campus access to online collections is only available to current UCLA students, faculty and staff.
