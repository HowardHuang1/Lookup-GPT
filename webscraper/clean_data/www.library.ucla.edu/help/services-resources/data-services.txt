About
The UCLA Library Data Science Center offers support in all stages of the data life cycle. Our services include consultation, training and documentation. Our team can help with the following:
Collecting, cleaning and organizing data
Managing data
Complying with funder requirements
Data visualization
Data analysis
Mapping and GIS
