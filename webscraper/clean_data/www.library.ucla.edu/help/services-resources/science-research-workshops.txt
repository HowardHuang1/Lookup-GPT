Workshop Series
Science Research Workshops
Science focused research skills and topics workshops, aimed at STEM audiences but open to all students.
More Information
About the Series
The Science Research Workshops are designed to help students learn about research topics within the sciences and develop research skills. Workshop topics cover a range of areas, including:
Understanding STEM resources
Using STEM databases
Funder compliance
Data management
Establishing scholarly identity within science fields
