A Commitment to discovery and learning
The professional and personal development of our medical students, graduate students, postdocs and trainees, and junior and senior faculty is paramount to accomplishing the overall mission of the David Geffen School of Medicine at UCLA, to improve Health and Healthcare.
Our dedicated faculty members are committed to providing excellence in teaching our trainees and we in turn are committed to providing excellence in learning and development.
