Community Engagement Projects
Encouraging and maintaining a healthy community is the foundation of our existence. At the David Geffen School of Medicine and UCLA Health, we are committed to supporting service learning, service delivery, education/workforce development, and community-based research projects. Our UCLA Community Engagement team is here to support our faculty, students and staff in their many efforts to build and strengthen presence in the community.
