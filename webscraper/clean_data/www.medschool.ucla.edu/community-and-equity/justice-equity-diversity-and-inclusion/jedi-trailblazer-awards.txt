About the JEDI Trailblazer Award
The JEDI Trailblazer Award is presented to those who embody leadership and excellence in advancing ideals of justice, equity, diversity, and inclusion.
This award is presented in collaboration with the AAPI Faculty Alliance and BLNA Collective (Black, Latinx and Native American Faculty Collective), Disability and Chronic Illness Group and PRIDE Network at the David Geffen School of Medicine at UCLA.
