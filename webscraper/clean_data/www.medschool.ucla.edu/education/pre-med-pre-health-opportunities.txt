Pre-Med & Pre-Health Pathway Program Opportunities
The Office of Outreach and Pathway Programs offers three pre-med and pre-health programs held annually to prepare promising pre-med students that experienced social, economic, or educational disadvantages for professional training and careers in medicine. Funding for these programs is provided by the University of California Academic Preparation & Educational Partnership, David Geffen School of Medicine.
Freshmen & Sophomore College Students
UCLA/DREW
Summer Health Professions Education Program
(SHPEP)
A six-week summer enrichment program for students who are interested in learning more about the health professions and how to apply to medical, nursing, and/or dental school.
Learn more
Junior & Senior College Students
UCLA
Pre Med Enrichment Program
(UCLA PREP)
A five-week summer program for students interested in strengthening their readiness to study medicine and apply to medical school.
Learn more
Post-Baccalaureate Students
UCLA
Re-Application Program
(UCLA RAP)
An eleven-month post-baccalaureate program designed to assist students who were previously unsuccessful in gaining admission to a U.S. medical school.
Learn more
