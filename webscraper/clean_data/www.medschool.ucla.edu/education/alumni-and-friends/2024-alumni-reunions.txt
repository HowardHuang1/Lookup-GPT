2024 Alumni Reunions
SAVE THE DATE!
DGSOM is celebrating the class reunions of '74, '84, '94, '99 & '04!
Join us on campus on October 19, 2024 for an evening on Switzer Plaza. Festivities will kick-off at 3 p.m. with pre-reception campus tours. The reunion reception starts at 4 p.m. with food, drinks, lawn games, and class fundraising competitions.
Campus Tours
Sign-up for a medical student-led tour of current Medical Education facilities. Tours will take place prior to the start of the reception.
Book Tour Times
Parking
Visitor parking is available for a day rate in Parking Lot 18, in the southeast corner of campus, adjacent to Switzer Plaza, Geffen Hall, and CHS.
Purchase Parking
Accommodations
The UCLA Luskin Conference Center in the heart of campus is offering a limited-time discounted rate for reunion guests 10/13 - 10/15.
Book by 9/1!
Reunion Class Fundraising
Making a gift to the Medial Alumni Association Class Scholarship in celebration of your reunion year is a meaningful way to give back to today's DGSOM community & support current students.
