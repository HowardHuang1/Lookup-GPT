Resources for Pathway Program Alumni
Requesting a Recommendation Letter
Please note that we submit the "Individual Letter" type of a letter of recommendation.
To request a letter of recommendation please fill out our request form. This will make the process of receiving a letter of recommendation more efficient.
Please make sure to be thorough when filling out the request, the more detail the stronger the letter of recommendation will be.
We need a minimum of 3 weeks for Letters of Recommendation. Unfortunately last minute requests will not be accommodated.
Letters of Recommendation cannot be accommodated during June, July, and August.
