The AAMC website provides resources such as what is on the MCAT exam, how to create a study plan, free planning and study resources, low cost products, and MCAT FAQs.&nbsp;</p>

<p><a href="https://students-residents.aamc.org/prepare-mcat-exam/practice-mcat-exam-official-low-cost-products"><strong>MCAT Resources from AAMC</strong></a><strong> (paid resource)</strong><br />
Low cost products from the AAMC such as MCAT Guides, practice question sets, full-length practice exams.</p>

<p><a href="https://www.khanacademy.org/test-prep/mcat"><strong>Khan Academy MCAT</strong></a><strong> (free resource)</strong><br />
A free online course that covers foundations of the MCAT and provides practice passages for various sections of the MCAT.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>

<p><strong><a href="https://gradschool.uworld.com/mcat/?gclid=CjwKCAjwkaSaBhA4EiwALBgQaIwaHiSaTD6t-pHDJQbY5TdcAaJNVp9I8j2DzT7eE4tNiHMzvqagzBoCVhUQAvD_BwE">Uworld MCAT Question Banks</a> (paid resource)</strong><br />

<p><a href="https://www.sketchy.com/explore/mcat?utm_term=&amp;utm_campaign=&amp;utm_source=adwords&amp;utm_medium=ppc&amp;hsa_acc=7031191013&amp;hsa_cam=18387591808&amp;hsa_grp=&amp;hsa_ad=&amp;hsa_src=x&amp;hsa_tgt=&amp;hsa_kw=&amp;hsa_mt=&amp;hsa_net=adwords&amp;hsa_ver=3&amp;gclid=CjwKCAjwkaSaBhA4EiwALBgQaJIUllgNsdwe2_pejSvhrYB4A3n_I1IPOc_i8qGVS1QLBnmzBFZc-BoCpYsQAvD_BwE"><strong>Sketchy MCAT</strong></a><strong> (paid resource)</strong><br />
A paid resource that has courses, review cards, and quiz questions geared towards connecting MCAT topics visually.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>

<p><a href="https://blueprintprep.com/mcat?lead_srcid=4947&amp;utm_source=google&amp;utm_medium=cpc&amp;utm_campaign=9715138313&amp;utm_term=%2Bblueprint%20%2Bmcat&amp;hsa_acc=5083186521&amp;hsa_cam=9715138313&amp;hsa_grp=100311591115&amp;hsa_ad=427889384120&amp;hsa_src=g&amp;hsa_tgt=kwd-891957399802&amp;hsa_kw=%2Bblueprint%20%2Bmcat&amp;hsa_mt=b&amp;hsa_net=adwords&amp;hsa_ver=3&amp;gclid=CjwKCAjwkaSaBhA4EiwALBgQaDBiH54JdnnKOATifyOxkL2Mu-iNuJqC5Kwf0dKRzhs8Kv5bwNr5jhoCoV0QAvD_BwE"><strong>Blueprint MCAT Full Length Test</strong></a><strong> (paid resource)</strong><br />
