Our Mission
The mission of the Financial Aid & Scholarships Office at the David Geffen School of Medicine at UCLA is to provide our medical school students with financial health resources to empower them to achieve their educational goals. We assist medical students in obtaining
medical school scholarships
and
loans
to help make medical education affordable for our talented and  diverse student body. We are also committed to empowering medical students to build their financial health through a robust curriculum focused on financial literacy and debt management. We are dedicated to providing high-quality, individualized support in a timely, supportive, confidential, and equitable manner.
