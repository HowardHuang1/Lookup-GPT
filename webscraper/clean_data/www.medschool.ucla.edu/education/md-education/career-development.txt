Find Your Career in Medicine
Support and community pave your way to success.
Did you know? There are over 135 specialties and subspecialties in medicine and many different types of practices.
Deciding which specialty to pursue is one of the most important decisions you will make during your time in medical school. It may feel daunting, but the Career Development team and many others at the David Geffen School of Medicine (DGSOM) at UCLA are here to help you explore careers in medicine.
Specialty Advisors for Career Exploration
Specialty Advisors for Career Exploration (Specialty ACEs) are faculty members passionate about mentoring students in their specialty.
Ace Specialties
Societies
The four societies at DGSOM provide Individualized guidance and support from your very first day of medical school until you graduate.
Societies
Career and Specialty Interest Groups (CSIGs)
CSIGs represent some of the 135 specialties and subspecialties that you can pursue through a residency or fellowship.
Career and Specialty Interest Groups
AAMC Careers in Medicine (CiM)
AAMC Careers in Medicine is a career planning program designed to help you choose a medical specialty and apply to a residency program.
