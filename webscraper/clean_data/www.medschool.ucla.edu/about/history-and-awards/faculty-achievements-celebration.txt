Faculty Achievements Celebration
DGSOM celebrates the extraordinary achievements of our outstanding faculty members who have been nationally and internally recognized for their contributions to medicine and science. Last year, DGSOM began this annual celebration to continue this tradition  to recognize and honor the great work of our faculty.
