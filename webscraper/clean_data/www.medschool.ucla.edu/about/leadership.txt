Executive Governing Group
Purpose Statement
To enhance communication and connectivity among the senior leadership of the David Geffen School of Medicine at UCLA. Approaching the work at hand with a collaborative lens we strive to build and maintain an inclusive, mission-driven culture by mapping our decisions, actions, and interactions to a shared framework.
