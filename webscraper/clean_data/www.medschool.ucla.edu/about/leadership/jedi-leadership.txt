JEDI Leadership
Justice, Equity, Diversity and Inclusion (JEDI)
Fostering an environment of respect that celebrates each individual’s perspectives, contributions, and expertise.
With the active support of its leadership, DGSOM is committed to recruiting and retaining outstanding students, residents, fellows, staff, and faculty from diverse backgrounds who represent the communities served by the University of California.
Our team champions inclusivity by engaging the entire DGSOM community of students, trainees, faculty, and staff in dialogues and activities.
Learn more about JEDI efforts at DGSOM
