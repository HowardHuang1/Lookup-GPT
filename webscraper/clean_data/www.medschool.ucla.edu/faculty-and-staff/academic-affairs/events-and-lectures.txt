Faculty Development Lecture Series Events
The virtual Faculty Development Lecture Series (FDLS) is targeted to the specific needs of faculty and is tailored to help them in their academic trajectory. The lectures are designed for both basic and clinical faculty.
From October to May, lectures are held monthly on the first Thursday of the month from 4:00 pm – 5:00 pm.
For more information, please contact
Cindy Cordova at
ccordova@mednet.ucla.edu
