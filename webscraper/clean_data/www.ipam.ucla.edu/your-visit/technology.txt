Technology
The Institute for Pure and Applied Mathematics Information Technology Group provides computing and
audio-visual services for IPAM participants. Dedicated dual-boot Windows/Linux workstations are furnished to resident researchers along with a wide array of pre-installed software, such as Matlab, Mathematica, assorted compilers, LaTeX, and general office applications. Campus wireless networks, including Eduroam are available to participants as are teleconferencing facilities and UCLA’s world-class digital libraries.
Technical assistance is available 8:30 to 4:30 by emailing the
IPAM Helpdesk
or calling (310) 825-8315. Our office is in IPAM room 1159E.
