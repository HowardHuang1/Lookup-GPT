CEE Partners Program
The UCLA-CEE Partners program facilitates interaction between participating organizations (design firms, contractors, private foundations, utilities, and government agencies) with CEE department students, staff, and faculty.
Participating organizations benefit through exclusive recruitment opportunities, faculty access, and research partnership opportunities.

Industrial Advisory Board
The Industry Advisory Board (IAB) provides support and guidance to the UCLA Civil and Environmental Engineering (CEE) Department from the perspective of industry leaders.
IAB provides valuable advice to CEE regarding the changing needs of industry and the engineering profession. IAB also provides suggestions for strengthening ties with industry in order to promote industry-academia interaction, joint collaboration and research, and technology transfer.
