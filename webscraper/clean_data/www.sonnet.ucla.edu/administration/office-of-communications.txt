Office of Communications
The Office of Communications at the UCLA School of Nursing is responsible for telling the UCLA Nursing story to the world. The Office of Communications provides a variety of support to Nursing faculty, staff and students, including content creation, website development, media relations, public relations, event support and more. In coordination with UCLA Strategic Communications, the Office of Communications works across the main campus and UCLA Health enterprise to elevate public awareness and perception of UCLA Nursing.
